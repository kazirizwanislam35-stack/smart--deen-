Al-Baqarah
Revealed: Madinah & Makkah (mixed) • Verses: 286 • Meaning: The Cow
1
الم
Alif-Lam-Mim
(Letters whose meaning is known to Allah.)
2
ذَٰلِكَ الْكِتَابُ لَا رَيْبَۛ فِيهِ هُدًى لِّلْمُتَّقِينَ
Dhālika al-kitābu lā rayba fīhi hudan lil-muttaqīn
This Book — no doubt in it — a guide for the God-conscious.
3
الَّذِينَ يُؤْمِنُونَ بِالْغَيْبِ وَيُقِيمُونَ الصَّلَاةَ وَمِمَّا رَزَقْنَاهُمْ يُنفِقُونَ
Alladhīna yu’minūna bil-ghaybi wa yuqīmūna aṣ-ṣalāh wa mimma razaqnāhum yunfiqūn
Those who believe in the unseen, keep prayer, and spend from what We have given them.
4
وَالَّذِينَ يُؤْمِنُونَ بِمَا أُنزِلَ إِلَيْكَ وَمَا أُنزِلَ مِن قَبْلِكَ وَبِالْآخِرَةِ هُمْ يُوقِنُونَ
Wa alladhīna yu’minūna bimā unzila ilayka wa bimā unzila min qablika wa bil-ākhirah hum yūqinūn
And who believe in revealed guidance to you and before you, and are certain of the Hereafter.
5
أُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ عَلَىٰ هُدًۭى مِّن رَّبِّهِمْ وَأُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ هُمُ الْمُفْلِحُونَ
Ulaa’ika ‘alā hudan min rabbihim wa ulaa’ika humul muflihūn
These are on guidance from their Lord — they are the successful.
6
إِنَّ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا سَوَاءٌ عَلَيْهِمْ أَأَنذَرْتَهُمْ أَمْ لَمْ تُنذِرْهُمْ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ
Inna alladhīna kafarū sawā’un ‘alayhim a’anzarta'hum am lam tunzirhum lā yu’minūn
Surely those who disbelieve — it is the same to them whether you warn them or not; they do not believe.
7
خَتَمَ اللَّهُ عَلَىٰ قُلُوبِهِمْ وَعَلَىٰ سَمْعِهِمْ وَعَلَىٰ أَبْصَـٰرِهِمْ غِشَـٰوَةٌ وَلَهُمْ عَذَابٌ عَظِيمٌ
Khatama allāhu ‘alā qulūbihim wa ‘alā sam’ihim wa ‘alā absārihim ghishāwātun wa lahum ‘adhābun ‘aẓīm
Allah has set a seal on their hearts and ears; they will have a great punishment.
8
وَمِنَ النَّاسِ مَن يَقُولُ آمَنَّا بِاللَّهِ وَبِالْيَوْمِ الْآخِرِ وَمَا هُم بِمُؤْمِنِينَ
Wa mina an-nāsi man yaqūlu āmannā bil-lāhi wa bil-yawmil ākhiri wa mā hum bimu’minīn
Among people are some who say, "We believe in Allah and the Last Day" — yet they are not believers.
9
يُخَادِعُونَ اللَّهَ وَالَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَمَا يَخْدَعُونَ إِلَّا أَنفُسَهُمْ وَمَا يَشْعُرُونَ
Yukhādi’ūna allāha wa alladhīna āmanū wa mā yakhda’ūna illā anfusahum wa mā yash’ūrūn
They try to deceive Allah and the believers, but they deceive only themselves without knowing.
10
فِي قُلُوبِهِم مَّرَضٌ فَزَادَهُمُ اللَّهُ مَرَضًا وَلَهُمْ عَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌ بِمَا كَانُوا يَكْذِبُونَ
Fī qulūbihim maraḍun fazādahumu allāhu maraḍan wa lahum ‘adhābun alīm bimā kānū yakdhubūn
In their hearts is disease; Allah increases them in disease — painful is their punishment for lying.
11
وَإِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمْ لَا تُفْسِدُوا فِي الْأَرْضِ قَالُوا إِنَّمَا نَحْنُ مُصْلِحُونَ
Wa idhā qīla lahum lā tufsidū fī al-arḍi qālū innamā naḥnu muṣliḥūn
When told not to corrupt the earth, they say, "We are only reformers."
12
أَلَا ۖ إِنَّهُمْ هُمُ الْمُفْسِدُونَ وَلَٰكِن لَّا يَشْعُرُونَ
Alā innahum humu al-mufsidūna walākin lā yash’ūrūn
Behold, they are the corrupters, though they perceive it not.
13
وَإِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمْ آمِنُوا كَمَا آمَنَ النَّاسُ قَالُوا أَنُؤْمِنُ كَمَا آمَنَ السُّفَهَاءُ ۗ أَلَا إِنَّهُمْ هُمُ السُّفَهَاءُ وَلَٰكِن لَّا يَعْلَمُونَ
Wa idhā qīla lahum āminū kamā āmana an-nās qālū anū’minu kamā āmana as-sufahā’u alā innahum humu as-sufahā’u walākin lā ya’lamūn
If asked to believe as others believe, they say, "Shall we believe as the foolish do?" Truly, they are the foolish though they know it not.
14
وَإِذَا لَقُوا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا قَالُوا آمَنَّا وَإِذَا خَلَوْا إِلَىٰ شَيَاطِينِهِمْ قَالُوا إِنَّا مَعَكُمْ إِنَّمَا نَحْنُ مُسْتَهْزِئُونَ
Wa idhā laqū alladhīna āmanū qālū āmannā wa idhā khalaw ilā shayāṭīnihim qālū innā ma’akum innamā naḥnu mustahzi’ūn
When meeting believers they say, "We believe"; but with their devils alone they scoff.
15
اللَّهُ يَسْتَهْزِئُ بِهِمْ وَيَمُدُّهُمْ فِي طُغْيَانِهِمْ يَعْمَهُونَ
Allāhu yastahzi'u bihim wa yamudduhum fī ṭugh'yānihim ya’mahūn
Allah lets them wander further in their error, wandering blindly.
16
أُولَـٰئِكَ الَّذِينَ اشْتَرَوُوا الضَّلَالَةَ بِالْهُدَىٰ فَمَا رَبِحَت تِّجَارَتُهُمْ وَمَا كَانُوا مُهْتَدِينَ
Ulaa’ika alladhīna ishtaraw aḍ-ḍalālata bil-hudā famā rabiḥat tijāratuhum wa mā kānū muhtadīn
They have exchanged guidance for error — their trade brings no profit; they are not rightly guided.
17
مَثَلُهُمْ كَمَثَلِ الَّذِي اسْتَوْقَدَ نَارًا فَلَمَّا أَضَاءَت مَا حَوْلَهُ ذَهَبَ اللَّهُ بِنُورِهِمْ وَتَرَكَهُمْ فِي ظُلُمَاتٍ لَا يُبْصِرُونَ
Mathaluhum kamathali alladhī istawqada nāran falammā aḍā'at mā ḥawlahū dhahaba allāhu binūrihim wa tarakahum fī ẓulumātin lā yubṣirūn
Their example is like one who lights a fire: when it lights what is around it, Allah takes away their light and leaves them in darkness where they cannot see.
18
صُمٌّ بُكْمٌ عُمْيٌ فَهُمْ لَا يَرْجِعُونَ
Ṣummun bukmun ‘umyun fahum lā yarji’ūn
Deaf, dumb, blind — they will not return to the right way.
19
أَوْ كَصَيِّبٍ مِّنَ السَّمَاءِ فِيهِ ظُلُمَاتٌ وَرَعْدٌ وَبَرْقٌ يَجْعَلُونَ أَصَابِعَهُمْ فِي آذَانِهِم مِّنَ الصَّوَاعِقِ حَذَرَ الْمَوْتِ وَاللَّهُ مُحِيطٌ بِالْكافِرِينَ
Aw kaṣayyibin mina as-samā’i fīhi ẓulumātun wa ra’dun wa barq yaj’aloona aṣābi’ahum fī ādhānihim mina aṣ-ṣawā’iq ḥadharul mawt wa Allāhu muḥīṭun bil-kāfirīn
Or like a storm from the sky with darkness, thunder and lightning; they put their fingers in their ears for fear of death. Allah encompasses the disbelievers.
20
يَكَادُ الْبَرْقُ يَخْطَفُ أَبْصَارَهُمْ كُلَّمَا أَضَاءَ لَهُم مَّشَوْا فِيهِ وَإِذَا أَظْلَمَ عَلَيْهِمْ قَامُوا ۚ وَلَوْ شَاءَ اللَّهُ لَذَهَبَ بِسَمْعِهِمْ وَأَبْصَارِهِمْ ۚ إِنَّ اللَّهَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ
Yakādu al-barqu yakhtafu abṣārahum kullamā aḍā’a lahum mashaw fīhi wa idhā aḍlama ‘alayhim qāmū wa law shā’a Allāhu ladhahaba bisam’ihim wa abṣārihim inna Allāha ‘alā kulli shay’in qadīr
Lightning almost snatches their sight; when it lights they walk, but when darkness comes they stand still. Allah is powerful over all things.
21
يَا أَيُّهَا النَّاسُ اعْبُدُوا رَبَّكُمُ الَّذِي خَلَقَكُمْ وَالَّذِينَ مِن قَبْلِكُمْ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَتَّقُونَ
Yā ayyuhān nāsu u’budū rabbakumu alladhī khalaqakum wa alladhīna min qablikum la’allakum tattaqūn
O people! Worship your Lord who created you and those before you, so you may be mindful (of Him).
22
الَّذِي جَعَلَ لَكُمُ الْأَرْضَ فِرَاشًا وَالسَّمَاء بِنَاءً وَأَنزَلَ مِنَ السَّمَاءِ مَاءً فَأَخْرَجَ بِهِ مِنَ الثَّمَرَاتِ رِزْقًا لَّكُمْ ۖ فَلَا تَجْعَلُوا لِلَّهِ أَندَادًا وَأَنتُمْ تَعْلَمُونَ
Alladhī ja’ala lakumul arḍa firāshan wa as-samā’a binā’an wa anzala mina as-samā’i mā’an fa akhraja bihi mina ath-thamārāti rizqan lakum fa lā taj’alū lillāhi andādan wa antum ta’lamūn
Who made the earth a resting place and the sky a roof, and sent down rain that produces fruits as provision — do not set partners with Allah when you know.
23
وَإِن كُنتُمْ فِي رَيْبٍ مِّمَّا نَزَّلْنَا عَلَىٰ عَبْدِنَا فَأْتُوا بِسُورَةٍ مِّن مِّثْلِهِ وَادْعُوا شُهَدَاءَكُم مِّن دُونِ اللَّهِ إِن كُنتُمْ صَادِقِينَ
Wa in kuntum fī raybin mimmā nazzalnā ‘alā ‘abdina fa’tū bisūratin mim mithlih wa ud’ū shuhadā’akum min dūni Allāhi in kuntum ṣādiqīn
If you doubt what We sent down, bring a chapter like it and call your witnesses besides Allah if you are truthful.
24
فَإِن لَّمْ تَفْعَلُوا وَلَن تَفْعَلُوا فَاتَّقُوا النَّارَ الَّتِي وَقُودُهَا النَّاسُ وَالْحِجَارَةُ أُعِدَّتْ لِلْكَافِرِينَ
Fa in lam taf’alū wa lan taf’alū fa ittaqū an-nār allati wa qūduhā an-nāsu wa al-ḥijāratu u’iddat lil-kāfirīn
If you cannot, then fear the Fire whose fuel is men and stones, prepared for disbelievers.
25
وَبَشِّرِ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ أَنَّ لَهُمْ جَنَّاتٍ تَجْرِي مِن تَحْتِهَا الْأَنْهَارُ ۖ كُلَّمَا رُزِقُوا مِنْهَا مِن ثَمَرَةٍ رِّزْقًا قَالُوا هَـٰذَا الَّذِي رُزِقْنَا مِن قَبْلُ وَأُوتُوا بِهِ مُتَشَابِهًا وَلَهُمْ فِيهَا أَزْوَاجٌ مُّطَهَّرَةٌ وَهُمْ فِيهَا خَالِدُونَ
Wa bashshir alladhīna āmanū wa ‘amilū aṣ-ṣāliḥāti anna lahum jannātin tajrī min taḥtihā al-anhāru kullamā ruziqū minhā min thamaraẗin rizqan qālū hādhā alladhī ruziqnā min qabl wa ūtū bihi mutashābihan wa lahum fīhā azwājun muṭahharah wa hum fīhā khālidūn
Give glad tidings to those who believe and do good: they will have gardens beneath which rivers flow; there they will have pure spouses and will dwell forever.
26
إِنَّ اللَّهَ لَا يَسْتَحْيِي أَنْ يَضْرِبَ مَثَلًا مَّا بَعُوضَةً فَمَا فَوْقَهَا ۚ فَأَمَّا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا فَيَعْلَمُونَ أَنَّهُ الْحَقُّ مِن رَّبِّهِمْ ۖ وَأَمَّا الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا فَيَقُولُونَ مَاذَا أَرَادَ اللَّهُ بِهَٰذَا مَثَلًا ۘ يُضِلُّ بِهِ كَثِيرًا وَيَهْدِي بِهِ كَثِيرًا ۚ وَمَا يُضِلُّ بِهِ إِلَّا الْفَاسِقِينَ
Inna Allāha lā yastahyī an yaḍriba mathalan mā ba’ūḍatan famā fawqahā fa ammā alladhīna āmanū faya’lamūna annahu al-ḥaqu min rabbihim wa ammā alladhīna kafarū fayaqūlūna mādhā arāda Allāhu bihādhā mathalan yuḍillu bihi kathīran wa yahdī bihi kathīran wa mā yuḍillu bihi illā al-fāsiqīn
Allah does not shy to set a parable, even a tiny insect; believers know it is truth from their Lord, while disbelievers ask, "What does Allah mean?" Parables both mislead and guide; they mislead only the perverse.
27
الَّذِينَ يَنقُضُونَ عَهْدَ اللَّهِ مِن بَعْدِ مِيثَاقِهِ وَيَقْطَعُونَ مَا أَمَرَ اللَّهُ بِهِ أَن يُوصَلَ وَيُفْسِدُونَ فِي الْأَرْضِ ۚ أُولَـٰئِكَ هُمُ الْخَاسِرُونَ
Alladhīna yanquḍūna ‘ahda Allāhi min ba’di mīthāqihi wa yaqṭa’ūna mā amara Allāhu bihi an yuṣala wa yufsidūna fī al-arḍi ulaa’ika humu al-khāsirūn
Those who break Allah's covenant after it was established, sever what Allah commanded to be joined, and spread corruption on earth — they are the losers.
28
كَيْفَ تَكْفُرُونَ بِاللَّهِ وَكُنتُمْ أَمْوَاتًا فَأَحْيَاكُمْ ۖ ثُمَّ يُمِيتُكُمْ ثُمَّ يُحْيِيكُمْ ثُمَّ إِلَيْهِ تُرْجَعُونَ
Kayfa takfuroona bil-lāhi wa kuntum amwātan fa aḥyākum thumma yumītukum thumma yuḥyīkum thumma ilayhi turja’ūn
How can you disbelieve in Allah when you were dead and He gave you life; then He will cause you to die again and give you life, then to Him you will return?
29
هُوَ الَّذِي خَلَقَ لَكُم مَّا فِي الْأَرْضِ جَمِيعًا ثُمَّ اسْتَوَىٰ إِلَى السَّمَاءِ فَسَوَّاهُنَّ سَبْعَ سَمَاوَاتٍ ۚ وَهُوَ بِكُلِّ شَيْءٍ عَلِيمٌ
Huwa alladhī khalaqa lakum mā fī al-arḍi jamī’an thumma stawā ilā as-samā’i fasawwāhunna sab’a samāwātin wa huwa bikulli shay’in ‘alīm
He created for you all that is on earth, then turned to the heavens and made them seven — He has knowledge of all things.
30
وَإِذْ قَالَ رَبُّكَ لِلْمَلَائِكَةِ إِنِّي جَاعِلٌ فِي الْأَرْضِ خَلِيفَةً ۖ قَالُوا أَتَجْعَلُ فِيهَا مَن يُفْسِدُ فِيهَا وَيَسْفِكُ الدِّمَاءَ وَنَحْنُ نُسَبِّحُ بِحَمْدِكَ وَنُقَدِّسُ لَكَ ۖ قَالَ إِنِّي أَعْلَمُ مَا لَا تَعْلَمُونَ
Wa idh qāla rabbuka lil-malā’ikati inī jā’ilun fī al-ardi khalīfatan qālū ataj’alu fīhā man yufsidu fīhā wa yasfiku ad-dimā’a wa naḥnu nusabbiḥu bi ḥamdika wa nuqaddisu laka qāla innī a’lamu mā lā ta’lamūn
When your Lord said to the angels, “I will place on earth a steward,” they asked, “Will You place one who spreads corruption and bloodshed, while we praise You?” He said, “I know what you do not.”
31
وَعَلَّمَ آدَمَ الْأَسْمَاءَ كُلَّهَا ثُمَّ عَرَضَهُمْ عَلَى الْمَلَائِكَةِ فَقَالَ أَنَبِّئُونِي بِأَسْمَاءِ هَٰؤُلَاءِ إِن كُنتُمْ صَادِقِينَ
Wa ‘allama ādamal asmā’a kullahā thumma ‘araḍahum ‘alā al-malā’ikati fa qāla anabbī’ūnī bi asmā’i hā’ulā’i in kuntum ṣādiqīn
He taught Adam the names of all things and presented them to the angels, asking them to inform Him of those names if they were truthful.
32
قَالَ أَنبِئُونِي بِأَسْمَائِهِمْ ۖ فَلَمَّا أَنبَأَهُم بِأَسْمَائِهِمْ قَالَ أَلَمْ أَقُل لَّكُمْ إِنِّي أَعْلَمُ غَيْبَ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ وَأَعْلَمُ مَا تُبْدُونَ وَمَا كُنتُمْ تَكْتُمُونَ
Qāla anbi’ūnī bi asmā’ihim fa lammā anba’ahum bi asmā’ihim qāla alam aqul lakum innī a’lamu ghayba as-samāwāti wal-ardi wa a’lamu mā tubdūna wa mā kuntum taktumulūn
When they told Him the names, He said, "Did I not tell you that I know the unseen of heavens and earth, and what you reveal and conceal?"
33
وَإِذْ قُلْنَا لِلْمَلَائِكَةِ اسْجُدُوا لِآدَمَ فَسَجَدُوا إِلَّا إِبْلِيسَ أَبَىٰ وَاسْتَكْبَرَ وَكَانَ مِنَ الْكَافِرِينَ
Wa idh qulna lil-malā’ikati usjudū li ādam fa sajadū illā ibleesa abā wa istakbara wa kāna mina al-kāfirīn
We commanded the angels to bow to Adam — they did, except Iblis, who refused and was among the disbelievers.
34
وَقُلْنَا يَا آدَمُ اسْكُنْ أَنتَ وَزَوْجُكَ الْجَنَّةَ وَكُلَا مِنْهَا رَغَدًا حَيْثُ شِئْتُمَا وَلَا تَقْرَبَا هَٰذِهِ الشَّجَرَةَ فَتَكُونَا مِنَ الظَّالِمِينَ
Wa qulna yā ādam uskunn anta wa zawjukal jannah wa kulā minhā raghadan ḥaythu shi’itmā wa lā taqrabā hādhihi ash-shajarata fata kunā min az-ẓālimīn
We said, "O Adam, dwell you and your spouse in Paradise and eat freely, but do not approach this tree, lest you be among the wrongdoers."
35
فَأَزَلَّهُمَا الشَّيْطَانُ عَنْهَا فَأَخْرَجَهُمَا مِمَّا كَانَا فِيهِ ۖ وَقُلْنَا اهْبِطُوا بَعْضُكُمْ لِبَعْضٍ عَدُوٌّ وَلَكُمْ فِي الْأَرْضِ مُسْتَقَرٌّ وَمَتَاعٌ إِلَىٰ حِينٍ
Fa azallahumā ash-shayṭānu ‘anhā fa akhrajahumā mimma kānā fīhi wa qulna ih’biṭū ba’ḍukum liba’ḍin ‘aduwwun wa lakum fī al-arḍi mustaqarrun wa matā’un ilā ḥīn
Satan caused them to slip and brought them out of what they were in. We said, "Descend — some of you to be enemies to others. You will have residence and provision on earth for a time."
36
فَلَقَدْ عَفَوْنَا لَهُمَا ۖ ثُمَّ اجْتَبَيْنَاهُمَا هُدًا ۚ وَلَقَدْ هَدَيْنَا آدَمَ ۖ وَجَعَلْنَا لَهُ مِنَ الْخَلْقِ ذُرِّيَّةً ۖ وَرَحْمَتِي وَسِعَتْ كُلَّ شَيْءٍ
Falaqad ‘afawnā lahumā thumma ijtabaynāhumā hudan wa laqad hadaynā ādama wa ja’alnā lahu mina al-khalqi dhurriyyatan wa raḥmatī wasi’at kulla shay’
We pardoned them, then guided them; We taught Adam and made his offspring — My mercy embraces all things.
37
إِذْ قَالَ رَبُّكَ لِلْمَلَائِكَةِ إِنِّي جَاعِلٌ فِي الْأَرْضِ خَلِيفَةً ۖ قَالُوا أَتَجْعَلُ فِيهَا مَن يُفْسِدُ فِيهَا وَيَسْفِكُ الدِّمَاءَ ۖ وَنَحْنُ نُسَبِّحُ بِحَمْدِكَ وَنُقَدِّسُ لَكَ ۖ قَالَ إِنِّي أَعْلَمُ مَا لَا تَعْلَمُونَ
[Repeat of earlier phrase — see Ayah 30]
(See earlier: Allah announced He would place a steward; angels questioned; Allah knows best.)
38
قَالَ اذْهَبُوا فَمَنْ تَبِعَكُم مِّنْهُمْ فَإِنِّي أَعِدُّ لَهُمْ نَارًا ۖ وَسَاءَتْ مَصِيرًا
Qāla idhhabū faman tabi'akum minhum fa innī a’iddul lahum nāran wa sā’at maṣīrā
He said, "Go forth; whoever follows you among them — I will prepare for them a Fire; an evil abode it is."
39
فَأَمَّا مَن تَابَ وَآمَنَ وَعَمِلَ صَالِحًا فَعَسَىٰ أَن يَكُونَ مِنَ الْمُفْلِحِينَ
Fa ammā man tāba wa āmana wa ‘amila ṣāliḥan fa ‘asā an yakūna mina al-muflihīn
But as for those who repent, believe and do good, they may be among the successful.
40
يَا بَنِي إِسْرَائِيلَ اذْكُرُوا نِعْمَتِيَ الَّتِي أَنْعَمْتُ عَلَيْكُمْ ۖ وَأَنِّي فَضَّلْتُكُمْ عَلَى الْعَالَمِينَ
Yā banī isrā'īla uthkurū ni’matī allatī an’amtu ‘alaykum wa annī faḍḍaltukum ‘alā al-’ālamīn
O Children of Israel! Remember My favor on you, and that I preferred you over the nations.
41
وَاتَّقُوا يَوْمًا لَا تَجْزِي نَفْسٌ عَنْ نَفْسٍ شَيْئًا وَلَا يُقْبَلُ مِنْهَا شَفَاعَةٌ وَلَا يُؤْخَذُ مِنْهَا عَدْلٌ وَلَا هُمْ يُنصَرُونَ
Wa ittaqū yawman lā tajzī nafsun 'an nafsin shay’an wa lā yuq'balu minhā shafā’ah wa lā yu’khathu minhā ‘adl wa lā hum yunsarūn
Fear the Day when no soul can avail another; no intercession will be accepted, nor compensation, nor will they be helped.
42
وَآتُوا الْمِكْيَالَ بِالْقِسْطِ وَلَا تُبْخِسُوا النَّاسَ أَشْيَاؤَهُمْ وَلَا تَعْثَوْا فِي الْأَرْضِ مُفْسِدِينَ
Wa ātū al-mikyāla bil-qisṭi wa lā tubkhisū an-nās a sh yā’ahum wa lā ta’thaw fī al-arḍi mufsidīn
Give full measure with justice; do not short people of their things and do not spread corruption on earth.
43
وَأَقِيمُوا الصَّلَاةَ وَآتُوا الزَّكَاةَ وَارْكَعُوا مَعَ الرَّاكِعِينَ
Wa aqīmū aṣ-ṣalāh wa ātū az-zakāh wa irka’ū maʿa ar-rākīn
Establish prayer, give zakah, and bow with those who bow (in worship).
44
أَتَأْمُرُونَ النَّاسَ بِالْبِرِّ وَتَنسَوْنَ أَنفُسَكُمْ وَأَنتُمْ تَتْلُونَ الْكِتَابَ ۚ أَفَلَا تَعْقِلُونَ
At-ta’murūna an-nās bil-birri wa tansawna anfusakum wa antum tatlūna al-kitāba afalā ta’qilūn
Do you enjoin righteousness on people while you neglect yourselves, yet you recite the Scripture? Will you not reason?
45
اتَّقُوا فِتْنَةً لَّا تُصِيبَنَّ الَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا مِنكُمْ خَاصَّةً ۖ وَاعْلَمُوا أَنَّ اللَّهَ شَدِيدُ الْعِقَابِ
Ittaqū fitnatan lā tuṣīban alladhīna ẓalamū minkum khāssatan wa i’lamū anna Allāha shadīdul ‘iqāb
Fear a trial that will not only strike the wrongdoers among you; know that Allah's punishment is severe.
46
وَاسْتَعِينُوا بِالصَّبْرِ وَالصَّلَاةِ ۚ وَإِنَّهَا لَكَبِيرَةٌ إِلَّا عَلَى الْخَاشِعِينَ
Wa ista’īnū biṣ-ṣabri wa aṣ-ṣalāh wa innahā lakabīrah illā ‘alā al-khāshi’īn
Seek help through patience and prayer — it is indeed heavy except for the humble.
47
يَا بَنِي إِسْرَائِيلَ اذْكُرُوا نِعْمَتِيَ الَّتِي أَنْعَمْتُ عَلَيْكُمْ وَأَنِّي فَضَّلْتُكُمْ عَلَى الْعَالَمِينَ
Yā banī isrā’īla uthkurū ni’matī allatī an’amtu ‘alaykum wa annī faḍḍaltukum ‘alā al-’ālamīn
O Children of Israel, remember My favor that I granted you and that I preferred you over the worlds.
48
وَاتَّقُوا يَوْمًا لَا تَجْزِي نَفْسٌ عَنْ نَفْسٍ شَيْئًا وَلَا يُقْبَلُ مِنْهَا شَفَاعَةٌ
Wa ittaqū yawman lā tajzī nafsun ‘an nafsin shay’an wa lā yuq'balu minhā shafā’ah
And fear the Day when no soul will avail another, nor will intercession be accepted.
49
أَفَلا يَعْقِلُونَ
Afalā ya’qilūn
Will they not understand?
50
وَكَذَٰلِكَ أَخْذْنَا بَنِي إِسْرَائِيلَ مِن بَعْدِ الْبَوْحِ مَا نَهَيْنَاهُمْ ۖ وَأَوْحَيْنَا إِلَيْكَ أَنْ أَنِ ٱذْكُرْ نِعْمَتِي عَلَيْكَ وَعَلَىٰ وَالِدَيْكَ
Wa kadhālika akhadhnā banī isrā’īla min ba’di al-bawḥi mā nahyaynāhum wa awḥaynā ilayka an an idhkur ni’matī ‘alayka wa ‘alā wālidayka
Thus We took a promise from the Children of Israel after the revelation, and We revealed to you to remember My favor upon you and your parents.
51
وَإِذْ وَاعَدْنَا مُوسَىٰ أَرْبَعِينَ لَيْلَةً ثُمَّ اتَّخَذْتُمُ الْعِجْلَ مِن بَعْدِهِ وَأَنتُمْ ظَالِمُونَ
Wa idh wāʿadnā Mūsā arbaʿīna laylatan thumma ittakhadhtum al-ʿijla min baʿdihi wa antum ẓālimūn
And when We appointed for Moses forty nights, then you took the calf after him while you were wrongdoers.
52
ثُمَّ عَفَوْنَا عَنكُم مِّنۢ بَعْدِ ذَٰلِكَ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَشْكُرُونَ
Thumma ʿafawnā ʿankum min baʿdi dhālika laʿallakum tashkurūn
Then We forgave you after that so that you might be grateful.
53
وَإِذْ آتَيْنَا مُوسَى الْكِتَابَ وَالْفُرْقَانَ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَهْتَدُونَ
Wa idh ātaynā Mūsā al-kitāba wal-furqāna laʿallakum tahtadūn
And when We gave Moses the Scripture and the Criterion so that you might be guided.
54
وَإِذْ قَالَ مُوسَىٰ لِقَوْمِهِۦ يَٰقَوْمِ إِنَّكُمْ ظَلَمْتُمْ أَنفُسَكُم بِٱتِّخَاذِكُمُ ٱلْعِجْلَ فَتُوبُوٓا۟ إِلَىٰ بَارِئِكُمْ فَاقْتُلُوٓا۟ أَنفُسَكُمْ ۖ ذَٰلِكُمْ خَيْرٌ لَّكُمْ عِندَ بَارِئِكُمْ ۖ فَتَابَ عَلَيْكُمْ ۚ إِنَّهُۥ هُوَ ٱلتَّوَّابُ ٱلرَّحِيمُ
Wa idh qāla Mūsā liqawmihi yā qawmi innakum ẓalamtum anfusakum bittikhādhikumu al-ʿijla fatūbū ilā bāriʾikum faqtulū anfusakum dhālikum khayrun lakum ʿinda bāriʾikum fatāba ʿalaykum innahu huwa at-tawwābu ar-raḥīm
And when Moses said to his people, “O my people, you have wronged yourselves by taking the calf, so turn in repentance to your Creator and kill yourselves. That is best for you with your Creator.” Then He accepted your repentance; indeed, He is the Accepting of Repentance, the Merciful.
55
وَإِذْ قُلْتُمْ يَٰمُوسَىٰ لَن نُّؤْمِنَ لَكَ حَتَّىٰ نَرَى ٱللَّهَ جَهْرَةً فَأَخَذَتْكُمُ ٱلصَّٰعِقَةُ وَأَنتُمْ تَنظُرُونَ
Wa idh qultum yā Mūsā lan nuʾmina laka ḥattā narā Allāha jahratan fa-akhadhatkumu aṣ-ṣāʿiqatu wa antum tanẓurūn
And when you said, “O Moses, we will never believe you until we see Allah outright,” then the thunderbolt struck you while you were looking on.
56
ثُمَّ بَعَثْنَٰكُم مِّنۢ بَعْدِ مَوْتِكُمْ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَشْكُرُونَ
Thumma baʿathnākum min baʿdi mawtikum laʿallakum tashkurūn
Then We revived you after your death so that you might be grateful.
57
وَظَلَّلْنَا عَلَيْكُمُ ٱلْغَمَامَ وَأَنزَلْنَا عَلَيْكُمُ ٱلْمَنَّ وَٱلسَّلْوَىٰ ۖ كُلُوا۟ مِن طَيِّبَٰتِ مَا رَزَقْنَٰكُمْ ۚ وَمَا ظَلَمُونَا وَلَٰكِن كَانُوٓا۟ أَنفُسَهُمْ يَظْلِمُونَ
Wa ẓallalnā ʿalaykumu al-ghamāma wa anzalnā ʿalaykumu al-manna wa as-salwā kulū min ṭayyibāti mā razaqnākum wa mā ẓalamūnā walākin kānū anfusahum yaẓlimūn
And We shaded you with clouds and sent down to you manna and quails. “Eat of the good things We have provided you.” They did not wrong Us, but they wronged themselves.
58
وَإِذْ قُلْنَا ٱدْخُلُوا۟ هَٰذِهِ ٱلْقَرْيَةَ فَكُلُوا۟ مِنْهَا حَيْثُ شِئْتُمْ رَغَدًا وَٱدْخُلُوا۟ ٱلْبَابَ سُجَّدًا وَقُولُوا۟ حِطَّةٌ نَّغْفِرْ لَكُمْ خَطَٰيَٰكُمْ ۚ وَسَنَزِيدُ ٱلْمُحْسِنِينَ
Wa idh qulna udkhulū hādhihi al-qaryata fakulū minhā ḥaythu shiʾtum raghadan wa udkhulū al-bāba sujjadan wa qūlū ḥiṭṭatun naghfir lakum khaṭāyākum wa sanazīdu al-muḥsinīn
And when We said, “Enter this town and eat freely from wherever you wish, and enter the gate bowing humbly and say, ‘Relieve us of our burdens,’ We will forgive your sins; and We will increase the doers of good.”
59
فَبَدَّلَ ٱلَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا۟ قَوْلًا غَيْرَ ٱلَّذِى قِيلَ لَهُمْ فَأَرْسَلْنَا عَلَيْهِمْ رِجْزًا مِّنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ بِمَا كَانُوا۟ يَظْلِمُونَ
Fa-baddala alladhīna ẓalamū qawlan ghayra alladhī qīla lahum fa-arsalnā ʿalayhim rijzan mina as-samāʾi bimā kānū yaẓlimūn
But those who wronged changed the word to something other than what had been said to them, so We sent upon them a punishment from the sky because they were wrongdoers.
60
وَإِذِ ٱسْتَسْقَىٰ مُوسَىٰ لِقَوْمِهِۦ فَقُلْنَا ٱضْرِب بِّعَصَاكَ ٱلْحَجَرَ ۖ فَٱنفَجَرَتْ مِنْهُ ٱثْنَتَا عَشْرَةَ عَيْنًا ۖ قَدْ عَلِمَ كُلُّ أُنَاسٍ مَّشْرَبَهُمْ ۖ كُلُوا۟ وَٱشْرَبُوا۟ مِن رِّزْقِ ٱللَّهِ وَلَا تَعْثَوْا۟ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ مُفْسِدِينَ
Wa idh istasqā Mūsā liqawmihi faqulnā idrib biʿaṣāka al-ḥajara fanfajarat minhu ithnatā ʿashrata ʿaynan qad ʿalima kullu unāsin mashrabahum kulū washrabū min rizqi Allāh wa lā taʿthaw fi al-arḍi mufsidīn
And when Moses prayed for water for his people, We said, “Strike the rock with your staff,” and there gushed forth twelve springs. Every people knew their drinking-place. “Eat and drink from the provision of Allah, and do not act wickedly on the earth.”
61
وَإِذْ قُلْتُمْ يَا مُوسَىٰ لَنْ نَصْبِرَ عَلَىٰ طَعَامٍ وَاحِدٍ فَادْعُ لَنَا رَبَّكَ يُخْرِجْ لَنَا مِمَّا تُنْبِتُ الْأَرْضُ ...
Wa idh qultum yā Mūsā lan naṣbira ʿalā ṭaʿāmin wāḥid...
And recall when you said, “O Moses! We cannot endure one kind of food. So pray to your Lord to bring forth for us what the earth produces...”
62
إِنَّ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَالَّذِينَ هَادُوا وَالنَّصَارَىٰ وَالصَّابِئِينَ ...
Inna alladhīna āmanū walladhīna hādū wa-n-naṣārā waṣ-ṣābi'īn...
Indeed, the believers, Jews, Christians, and Sabians—whoever believes in Allah and the Last Day and does good—will have their reward with their Lord...
63
وَإِذْ أَخَذْنَا مِيثَاقَكُمْ وَرَفَعْنَا فَوْقَكُمُ الطُّورَ ...
Wa idh akhadhnā mīthāqakum wa rafaʿnā fawqakumu ṭ-ṭūr...
And when We took your covenant and raised the mount above you...
64
ثُمَّ تَوَلَّيْتُمْ مِنْ بَعْدِ ذَٰلِكَ ۖ فَلَوْلَا فَضْلُ اللَّهِ عَلَيْكُمْ ...
Thumma tawallaytum min baʿdi dhālik fa-lawlā faḍlullāhi ʿalaykum...
Then you turned away afterward. If not for Allah’s grace and mercy upon you, you would have been among the losers.
65
وَلَقَدْ عَلِمْتُمُ الَّذِينَ اعْتَدَوْا مِنْكُمْ فِي السَّبْتِ ...
Wa laqad ʿalimtumu alladhīna ʿtadaw minkum fi s-sabt...
You surely knew those among you who violated the Sabbath, so We said, “Be despised apes.”
66
فَجَعَلْنَاهَا نَكَالًا لِمَا بَيْنَ يَدَيْهَا وَمَا خَلْفَهَا ...
Fa-jaʿalnāhā nakālan limā bayna yadayhā wa-mā khalfahā...
So We made this a lesson for those who were present and for those to follow, and a reminder for the righteous.
67
وَإِذْ قَالَ مُوسَىٰ لِقَوْمِهِ إِنَّ اللَّهَ يَأْمُرُكُمْ أَنْ تَذْبَحُوا بَقَرَةً ...
Wa idh qāla Mūsā li-qawmihi inna Allāha ya'murukum an tadhbaḥū baqarah...
And when Moses said to his people, “Allah commands you to sacrifice a cow.” They said, “Do you take us in ridicule?”
68
قَالُوا ادْعُ لَنَا رَبَّكَ يُبَيِّنْ لَنَا مَا هِيَ ...
Qālū idʿu lanā rabbaka yubayyin lanā mā hiya...
They said, “Call upon your Lord to clarify to us what it is.”
69
قَالُوا ادْعُ لَنَا رَبَّكَ يُبَيِّنْ لَنَا مَا لَوْنُهَا ...
Qālū idʿu lanā rabbaka yubayyin lanā mā lawnuhā...
They said, “Call upon your Lord to clarify to us its color.”
70
قَالُوا ادْعُ لَنَا رَبَّكَ يُبَيِّنْ لَنَا مَا هِيَ ۚ إِنَّ الْبَقَرَ تَشَابَهَ عَلَيْنَا ...
Qālū idʿu lanā rabbaka yubayyin lanā mā hiya inna al-baqar tashābah ʿalaynā...
They said, “Call upon your Lord to clarify to us what it is. To us, all cows look alike...”
71
قَالَ إِنَّهُ يَقُولُ إِنَّهَا بَقَرَةٌ لَا ذَلُولٌ تُثِيرُ الْأَرْضَ وَلَا تَسْقِي الْحَرْثَ مُسَلَّمَةٌ لَا شِيَةَ فِيهَا ۚ قَالُوا الْآنَ جِئْتَ بِالْحَقِّ ۚ فَذَبَحُوهَا وَمَا كَادُوا يَفْعَلُونَ
Qāla innahu yaqūlu innahā baqaratun lā dhulūlun tuthīrul-arḍa walā tasqī al-ḥarth, musallamatun lā shiyata fīhā. Qālū al-āna ji’ta bil-ḥaqq. Fa-dhabḥūhā wa mā kādū yafʿalūn.
He said, “Allah says it is a cow neither trained for plowing nor watering the field, sound and without blemish.” They said, “Now you have brought the truth.” So they slaughtered it, though they hardly wished to do so.
72
وَإِذْ قَتَلْتُمْ نَفْسًا فَادَّارَأْتُمْ فِيهَا ۖ وَاللَّهُ مُخْرِجٌ مَا كُنْتُمْ تَكْتُمُونَ
Wa idh qataltum nafsan fa-ddarā’tum fīhā, wallāhu mukhrijun mā kuntum taktumūn.
And recall when you killed a man and disputed about it, but Allah was to reveal what you concealed.
73
فَقُلْنَا اضْرِبُوهُ بِبَعْضِهَا ۚ كَذَٰلِكَ يُحْيِ اللَّهُ الْمَوْتَىٰ وَيُرِيكُمْ آيَاتِهِ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَعْقِلُونَ
Fa-qulnā iḍribūhu bi-baʿḍihā. Kadhālika yuḥyillāhu al-mawtā wa yurīkum āyātihī laʿallakum taʿqilūn.
So We said, “Strike the slain man with part of it.” Thus Allah brings the dead to life and shows you His signs so you may understand.
74
ثُمَّ قَسَتْ قُلُوبُكُمْ مِنْ بَعْدِ ذَٰلِكَ فَهِيَ كَالْحِجَارَةِ أَوْ أَشَدُّ قَسْوَةً...
Thumma qasat qulūbukum min baʿdi dhālik fa-hiya kal-ḥijārati aw ashaddu qaswah...
Then your hearts became hardened after that—like stones or even harder...
75
أَفَتَطْمَعُونَ أَنْ يُؤْمِنُوا لَكُمْ وَقَدْ كَانَ فَرِيقٌ مِنْهُمْ...
A-fa-taṭmaʿūna an yu’minū lakum wa qad kāna farīqun minhum...
Do you then hope they would believe you while a group of them used to hear Allah’s word and then alter it knowingly?
76
وَإِذَا لَقُوا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا قَالُوا آمَنَّا...
Wa idhā laqū alladhīna āmanū qālū āmannā...
And when they meet the believers, they say, “We believe,” but when alone with one another they say, “Do you reveal to them what Allah has disclosed to you?”
77
أَوَلَا يَعْلَمُونَ أَنَّ اللَّهَ يَعْلَمُ مَا يُسِرُّونَ وَمَا يُعْلِنُونَ
A-wa lā yaʿlamūna anna Allāha yaʿlamu mā yusirrūna wa mā yuʿlinūn.
Do they not know that Allah knows what they conceal and what they reveal?
78
وَمِنْهُمْ أُمِّيُّونَ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ الْكِتَابَ...
Wa minhum ummiyyūna lā yaʿlamūna al-kitāba illā amāniyy...
Among them are unlettered people who know nothing of the Book except wishful thinking...
79
فَوَيْلٌ لِلَّذِينَ يَكْتُبُونَ الْكِتَابَ بِأَيْدِيهِمْ...
Fa-waylun li-lladhīna yaktubūna al-kitāba bi-aydīhim...
Woe to those who write the Book with their own hands then say, “This is from Allah,” to gain a small profit!
80
وَقَالُوا لَنْ تَمَسَّنَا النَّارُ إِلَّا أَيَّامًا مَعْدُودَةً...
Wa qālū lan tamassanā an-nāru illā ayyāman maʿdūdah...
They say, “The Fire will only touch us for a few days.” Say, “Have you taken a promise from Allah?”
81
بَلَىٰ مَنْ كَسَبَ سَيِّئَةً وَأَحَاطَتْ بِهِ خَطِيئَتُهُ...
Balā man kasaba sayyi’atan wa aḥāṭat bihī khaṭī’atuh...
Yes! Whoever commits evil and is surrounded by sin—those are the companions of the Fire...
82
وَالَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ...
Wa alladhīna āmanū wa ʿamilū ṣ-ṣāliḥāt...
But those who believe and do good deeds—they are the companions of Paradise, they will remain there forever.
83
وَإِذْ أَخَذْنَا مِيثَاقَ بَنِي إِسْرَائِيلَ لَا تَعْبُدُونَ إِلَّا اللَّهَ...
Wa idh akhadhnā mīthāqa banī Isrā’īla lā taʿbudūna illā Allāh...
And when We took the covenant from the Children of Israel: “Worship none but Allah...”
84
وَإِذْ أَخَذْنَا مِيثَاقَكُمْ لَا تَسْفِكُونَ دِمَاءَكُمْ...
Wa idh akhadhnā mīthāqakum lā tasfikūna dimā’akum...
And when We took your covenant: “Do not shed your own blood or expel one another from your homes.”
85
ثُمَّ أَنْتُمْ هَٰؤُلَاءِ تَقْتُلُونَ أَنْفُسَكُمْ...
Thumma antum hā’ulā’i taqtulūna anfusakum...
Then you are the same people killing one another and expelling a group of your own from their homes...
86
أُولَٰئِكَ الَّذِينَ اشْتَرَوُا الْحَيَاةَ الدُّنْيَا بِالْآخِرَةِ...
Ulā’ika alladhīna ishtaraw al-ḥayāta ad-dunyā bil-ākhirah...
Those are the ones who have purchased the worldly life at the price of the Hereafter...
87
وَلَقَدْ آتَيْنَا مُوسَى الْكِتَابَ وَقَفَّيْنَا مِنْ بَعْدِهِ بِالرُّسُلِ...
Wa laqad ātaynā Mūsā al-kitāba wa qaffaynā min baʿdihī bir-rusul...
We gave Moses the Book and followed him with messengers. And We gave Jesus clear proofs and supported him with the Holy Spirit...
88
وَقَالُوا قُلُوبُنَا غُلْفٌ ۚ بَلْ لَعَنَهُمُ اللَّهُ بِكُفْرِهِمْ...
Wa qālū qulūbunā ghulf. Bal laʿanahumullāhu bi-kufrihim...
They said, “Our hearts are sealed.” But Allah has cursed them for their disbelief...
89
وَلَمَّا جَاءَهُمْ كِتَابٌ مِنْ عِنْدِ اللَّهِ مُصَدِّقٌ لِمَا مَعَهُمْ...
Wa lammā jā’ahum kitābun min ʿindi Allāhi muṣaddiqun limā maʿahum...
And when a Book came to them from Allah confirming what was with them... they disbelieved in it.
90
بِئْسَمَا اشْتَرَوْا بِهِ أَنْفُسَهُمْ...
Bi’samā ishtaraw bihī anfusahum...
How terrible is that for which they sold their souls—to deny what Allah revealed out of envy...
91
وَإِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمْ آمِنُوا بِمَا أَنْزَلَ ٱللَّهُ قَالُوا نُؤْمِنُ بِمَا أُنْزِلَ عَلَيْنَا وَيَكْفُرُونَ بِمَا وَرَاءَهُ ۖ وَهُوَ ٱلْحَقُّ مُصَدِّقًا لِمَا مَعَهُمْ ۗ قُلْ فَلِمَ تَقْتُلُونَ أَنْبِيَاءَ ٱللَّهِ مِنْ قَبْلُ إِنْ كُنْتُمْ مُؤْمِنِينَ
Wa idhā qīla lahum āminū bimā anzala Allāhu qālū nu’minu bimā unzila ‘alaynā wa yakfurūna bimā warā’ahu wa huwa al-ḥaqqu muṣaddiqan limā ma‘ahum qul fa lima taqtulūna anbiyā’a Allāhi min qablu in kuntum mu’minīn
When it is said to them, “Believe in what Allah has revealed,” they say, “We believe in what was revealed to us,” but they reject what came after, even though it is the truth confirming what they have. Say, “Why then did you kill the prophets of Allah before, if you were believers?”
92
وَلَقَدْ جَاءَكُمْ مُوسَىٰ بِٱلْبَيِّنَاتِ ثُمَّ ٱتَّخَذْتُمُ ٱلْعِجْلَ مِنْ بَعْدِهِ ۖ وَأَنْتُمْ ظَالِمُونَ
Wa laqad jā’akum Mūsā bil-bayyināti thumma ittakhdhtumu al-‘ijla min ba‘dihi wa antum ẓālimūn
Moses came to you with clear signs, then after he left you worshipped the calf while you were wrongdoers.
93
وَإِذْ أَخَذْنَا مِيثَاقَكُمْ وَرَفَعْنَا فَوْقَكُمُ ٱلطُّورَ ...
Wa idh akhadhnā mīthāqakum wa rafa‘nā fawqakumu al-ṭūr...
And when We took your covenant and raised the mountain above you, saying, “Hold firmly to what We have given you and listen”...
94
قُلْ إِنْ كَانَتْ لَكُمُ ٱلدَّارُ ٱلْآخِرَةُ عِنْدَ ٱللَّهِ خَالِصَةً مِنْ دُونِ ٱلنَّاسِ فَتَمَنَّوُا الْمَوْتَ إِنْ كُنْتُمْ صَادِقِينَ
Qul in kānat lakumu al-dāru al-ākhiratu ‘inda Allāhi khāliṣatan min dūni al-nāsi fatamannawū al-mawta in kuntum ṣādiqīn
Say, “If the Home of the Hereafter with Allah is exclusively for you and not for others, then wish for death if you are truthful.”
95
وَلَنْ يَتَمَنَّوْهُ أَبَدًا بِمَا قَدَّمَتْ أَيْدِيهِمْ ۚ وَاللَّهُ عَلِيمٌ بِالظَّالِمِينَ
Wa lan yatamannawhu abadan bimā qaddamat aydīhim wa Allāhu ‘alīmun bi al-ẓālimīn
But they will never wish for it, because of what their hands have sent forth. And Allah knows the wrongdoers.
96
وَلَتَجِدَنَّهُمْ أَحْرَصَ النَّاسِ عَلَىٰ حَيَاةٍ ...
Wa latajidannahum aḥraṣa al-nāsi ‘alā ḥayāh...
And you will surely find them the most eager of all people for life, even more than those who associate others with Allah...
97
قُلْ مَنْ كَانَ عَدُوًّا لِجِبْرِيلَ فَإِنَّهُ نَزَّلَهُ عَلَىٰ قَلْبِكَ بِإِذْنِ ٱللَّهِ ...
Qul man kāna ‘aduwwan li-Jibrīla fa innahu nazzalahu ‘alā qalbika bi idhni Allāh...
Say, “Whoever is an enemy to Gabriel—he it is who has brought it down to your heart by Allah’s permission...”
98
مَنْ كَانَ عَدُوًّا لِلَّهِ وَمَلَائِكَتِهِ وَرُسُلِهِ وَجِبْرِيلَ وَمِيكَائِيلَ ...
Man kāna ‘aduwwan lillāhi wa malā’ikatihi wa rusulihi wa Jibrīla wa Mīkā’īla...
Whoever is an enemy to Allah and His angels and His messengers and Gabriel and Michael—then Allah is an enemy to the disbelievers.
99
وَلَقَدْ أَنْزَلْنَا إِلَيْكَ آيَاتٍ بَيِّنَاتٍ ۖ وَمَا يَكْفُرُ بِهَا إِلَّا الْفَاسِقُونَ
Wa laqad anzalnā ilayka āyātin bayyinātin wa mā yakfuru bihā illā al-fāsiqūn
We have certainly sent down to you clear verses, and none disbelieve in them except the defiantly disobedient.
100
أَوَ كُلَّمَا عَاهَدُوا عَهْدًا نَبَذَهُ فَرِيقٌ مِنْهُمْ ۚ بَلْ أَكْثَرُهُمْ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ
Awa kullamā ‘āhadū ‘ahdan nabadhahu farīqun minhum bal aktharuhum lā yu’minūn
Is it not that whenever they made a covenant, a party of them threw it away? Rather, most of them do not believe.
101
وَلَمَّا جَاءَهُمْ رَسُولٌ مِنْ عِنْدِ ٱللَّهِ مُصَدِّقٌ لِمَا مَعَهُمْ نَبَذَ فَرِيقٌ مِّنَ ٱلَّذِينَ أُوتُوا ٱلْكِتَابَ كِتَابَ ٱللَّهِ وَرَاءَ ظُهُورِهِمْ كَأَنَّهُمْ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ
Wa lammā jā’ahum rasūlun min ‘indi Allāhi muṣaddiqun limā ma‘ahum nabadha farīqun mina alladhīna ūtū al-kitāba kitāba Allāhi warā’a ẓuhūrihim ka’annahum lā ya‘lamūn
And when a messenger from Allah came to them confirming what was with them, a group of those who had been given the Scripture threw the Book of Allah behind their backs as if they did not know.
102
وَاتَّبَعُوا مَا تَتْلُوا ٱلشَّيَاطِينُ عَلَىٰ مُلْكِ سُلَيْمَانَ ۖ ...
Wa ittaba‘ū mā tatlū al-shayāṭīnu ‘alā mulki Sulaymāna...
They followed what the devils recited during the reign of Solomon. Solomon did not disbelieve, but the devils disbelieved...
103
وَلَوْ أَنَّهُمْ ءَامَنُوا وَٱتَّقَوْا لَمَثُوبَةٌ مِّنْ عِندِ ٱللَّهِ خَيْرٌ ۖ لَّوْ كَانُوا يَعْلَمُونَ
Walaw annahum āmanū wa ittaqaw la-mathūbatun min ‘indi Allāhi khayrun law kānū ya‘lamūn
And if they had believed and feared Allah, then the reward from Allah would have been better, if only they knew.
104
يَا أَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا لَا تَقُولُوا رَاعِنَا وَقُولُوا ٱنْظُرْنَا وَٱسْمَعُوا ۗ ...
Yā ayyuhā alladhīna āmanū lā taqūlū rā‘inā wa qūlū unẓurnā wa isma‘ū...
O believers! Do not say “Rā‘inā,” but say “Unẓurnā,” and listen. And for the disbelievers is a painful punishment.
105
مَا يَوَدُّ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا مِنْ أَهْلِ ٱلْكِتَابِ وَلَا ٱلْمُشْرِكِينَ أَنْ يُنَزَّلَ عَلَيْكُمْ مِنْ خَيْرٍ مِنْ رَبِّكُمْ ۚ ...
Mā yawaddu alladhīna kafarū min ahli al-kitābi wa lā al-mushrikīna an yunazzala ‘alaykum min khayrin min rabbikum...
Neither the disbelievers of the People of the Book nor the polytheists like that any good should be sent down to you from your Lord...
106
مَا نَنْسَخْ مِنْ ءَايَةٍ أَوْ نُنْسِهَا نَأْتِ بِخَيْرٍ مِنْهَا ...
Mā nansakh min āyatin aw nunsihā na’ti bikhayrin minhā...
We do not abrogate a verse or cause it to be forgotten except that We bring one better than it or similar to it...
107
أَلَمْ تَعْلَمْ أَنَّ ٱللَّهَ لَهُۥ مُلْكُ ٱلسَّمَٰوَاتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ ...
A lam ta‘lam anna Allāha lahu mulku al-samāwāti wa al-arḍ...
Do you not know that to Allah belongs the dominion of the heavens and the earth?
108
أَمْ تُرِيدُونَ أَنْ تَسْـَٔلُوا رَسُولَكُمْ كَمَا سُئِلَ مُوسَىٰ مِنْ قَبْلُ ۗ ...
Am turīdūna an tas’alū rasūlakum kamā su’ila Mūsā min qablu...
Do you wish to question your messenger as Moses was questioned before?
109
وَدَّ كَثِيرٌ مِّنْ أَهْلِ ٱلْكِتَابِ لَوْ يَرُدُّونَكُمْ ...
Wadda kathīrun min ahli al-kitābi law yaruddūnakum...
Many among the People of the Book wish they could turn you back to disbelief after your belief...
110
وَأَقِيمُوا ٱلصَّلَوٰةَ وَءَاتُوا ٱلزَّكَوٰةَ ۚ وَمَا تُقَدِّمُوا لِأَنفُسِكُم ...
Wa aqīmū al-ṣalāta wa ātū al-zakāh wa mā tuqaddimū li anfusikum...
Establish prayer and give zakāh, and whatever good you put forward for yourselves—you will find it with Allah.
111
وَقَالُوا لَن يَدْخُلَ ٱلْجَنَّةَ إِلَّا مَن كَانَ هُودًا أَوْ نَصَارَىٰ ...
Wa qālū lan yadkhula al-jannata illā man kāna hūdan aw naṣārā...
They say, “None will enter Paradise unless he be a Jew or a Christian.” These are their wishes...
112
بَلَىٰ مَنْ أَسْلَمَ وَجْهَهُۥ لِلَّهِ وَهُوَ مُحْسِنٌ ...
Balā man aslama wajhahu lillāhi wa huwa muḥsinun...
Yes—whoever submits his face to Allah and is righteous will have his reward with his Lord.
113
وَقَالَتِ ٱلْيَهُودُ لَيْسَتِ ٱلنَّصَارَىٰ عَلَىٰ شَىْءٍ ...
Wa qālati al-yahūdu laysati al-naṣārā ‘alā shay’in...
The Jews say, “The Christians have nothing,” and the Christians say, “The Jews have nothing,” yet they recite the Scripture...
114
وَمَنْ أَظْلَمُ مِمَّن مَّنَعَ مَسَٰجِدَ ٱللَّهِ أَن يُذْكَرَ فِيهَا ٱسْمُهُۥ ...
Wa man aẓlamu mimman mana‘a masājida Allāhi an yudhkara fīhā ismuhu...
And who is more unjust than one who prevents Allah’s name from being mentioned in His mosques...
115
وَلِلَّهِ ٱلْمَشْرِقُ وَٱلْمَغْرِبُ ۚ فَأَيْنَمَا تُوَلُّوا فَثَمَّ وَجْهُ ٱللَّهِ ۚ ...
Wa lillāhi al-mashriqu wa al-maghrib fa aynamā tuwallū fathamma wajhu Allāh...
To Allah belong the East and the West. Wherever you turn, there is the Face of Allah.
116
وَقَالُوا۟ ٱتَّخَذَ ٱللَّهُ وَلَدًۭا ۗ سُبْحَٰنَهُۥ ۖ بَل لَّهُۥ مَا فِى ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ ۖ كُلٌّۭ لَّهُۥ قَٰنِتُونَ
Wa qālū ittakhadha Allāhu waladā, subḥānahu, bal lahu mā fī al-samāwāti wa al-arḍ, kullun lahu qānitūn.
They say, “Allah has taken a son.” Glory be to Him! Everything in the heavens and the earth belongs to Him; all are obedient to Him.
117
بَدِيعُ ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ ۖ وَإِذَا قَضَىٰٓ أَمْرًۭا فَإِنَّمَا يَقُولُ لَهُۥ كُن فَيَكُونُ
Badīʿu al-samāwāti wa al-arḍ, wa idhā qaḍā amran fa innamā yaqūlu lahu kun fa-yakūn.
He is the Originator of the heavens and the earth. When He decrees a matter, He only says to it, “Be,” and it is.
118
وَقَالَ ٱلَّذِينَ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ لَوْلَا يُكَلِّمُنَا ٱللَّهُ أَوْ تَأْتِينَآ ءَايَةٌۭ ۗ ...
Wa qāla alladhīna lā yaʿlamūna lawlā yukallimunā Allāhu aw ta’tīnā āyah...
Those who do not know say, “Why does Allah not speak to us, or a sign come to us?”...
119
إِنَّآ أَرْسَلْنَٰكَ بِٱلْحَقِّ بَشِيرًۭا وَنَذِيرًۭا ۖ وَلَا تُسْـَٔلُ عَنْ أَصْحَٰبِ ٱلْجَحِيمِ
Innā arsalnāka bi-al-ḥaqqi bashīran wa nadhīran, wa lā tus’alu ʿan aṣḥābi al-jaḥīm.
Indeed, We have sent you with the truth as a bearer of good news and a warner. And you will not be questioned about the people of Hell.
120
وَلَن تَرْضَىٰ عَنكَ ٱلْيَهُودُ وَلَا ٱلنَّصَٰرَىٰ حَتَّىٰ تَتَّبِعَ مِلَّتَهُمْ ۗ ...
Wa lan tarḍā ʿanka al-yahūdu wa lā al-naṣārā ḥattā tattabiʿa millatahum...
The Jews and Christians will never be pleased with you until you follow their religion...
121
ٱلَّذِينَ ءَاتَيْنَٰهُمُ ٱلْكِتَٰبَ يَتْلُونَهُۥ حَقَّ تِلَاوَتِهِۦٓ ۙ ...
Alladhīna ātaynāhumu al-kitāba yatlūnahu ḥaqqa tilāwatih...
Those to whom We have given the Book recite it as it should be recited...
122
يَٰبَنِىٓ إِسْرَٰٓءِيلَ ٱذْكُرُوا۟ نِعْمَتِىَ ٱلَّتِىٓ أَنْعَمْتُ عَلَيْكُمْ ...
Yā banī isrā’īla udhkurū niʿmatī allatī anʿamtu ʿalaykum...
O Children of Israel, remember My favor which I bestowed upon you...
123
وَٱتَّقُوا۟ يَوْمًۭا لَّا تَجْزِى نَفْسٌ عَن نَّفْسٍۢ شَيْـًۭٔا ...
Wa ittaqū yawman lā tajzī nafsun ʿan nafsin shay’ā...
And fear a Day when no soul shall avail another in anything...
124
وَإِذِ ٱبْتَلَىٰٓ إِبْرَٰهِـۧمَ رَبُّهُۥ بِكَلِمَٰتٍۢ فَأَتَمَّهُنَّ ۖ ...
Wa idh ibtalā ibrāhīma rabbuhu bi-kalimātin fa atammahunna...
And when Abraham was tested by his Lord with certain commands, and he fulfilled them...
125
وَإِذْ جَعَلْنَا ٱلْبَيْتَ مَثَابَةًۭ لِّلنَّاسِ وَأَمْنًۭا ...
Wa idh jaʿalnā al-bayta mathābatan li-al-nās wa amnā...
And when We made the House a place of return for people and a sanctuary...
126
وَإِذْ قَالَ إِبْرَٰهِـۧمُ رَبِّ ٱجْعَلْ هَـٰذَا بَلَدًا ءَامِنًۭا ...
Wa idh qāla ibrāhīmu rabbi ijʿal hādhā baladan āminā...
And when Abraham said, “My Lord, make this a secure city...”
127
وَإِذْ يَرْفَعُ إِبْرَٰهِـۧمُ ٱلْقَوَاعِدَ مِنَ ٱلْبَيْتِ وَإِسْمَـٰعِيلُ ...
Wa idh yarfaʿu ibrāhīmu al-qawāʿida mina al-bayti wa ismaʿīl...
And when Abraham raised the foundations of the House, along with Ishmael...
128
رَبَّنَا وَٱجْعَلْنَا مُسْلِمَيْنِ لَكَ ...
Rabbana wajʿalnā muslimayn laka...
Our Lord, make us both submissive to You, and from our descendants a nation submissive to You...
129
رَبَّنَا وَٱبْعَثْ فِيهِمْ رَسُولًۭا مِّنْهُمْ ...
Rabbana wabʿath fīhim rasūlan minhum...
Our Lord, raise among them a messenger from themselves who will recite to them Your verses...
130
وَمَن يَرْغَبُ عَن مِّلَّةِ إِبْرَٰهِـۧمَ إِلَّا مَن سَفِهَ نَفْسَهُۥ ...
Wa man yarghabu ʿan millati ibrāhīma illā man safiha nafsah...
Who would turn away from the religion of Abraham except one who makes himself foolish?
131
إِذْ قَالَ لَهُۥ رَبُّهُۥٓ أَسْلِمْ ۖ قَالَ أَسْلَمْتُ لِرَبِّ ٱلْعَـٰلَمِينَ
Idh qāla lahu rabbuhu aslim qāla aslamtu li rabbil-ʿālamīn
When his Lord said to him, “Submit,” he replied, “I have submitted to the Lord of the worlds.”
132
وَوَصَّىٰ بِهَآ إِبْرَٰهِـۧمُ بَنِيهِ وَيَعْقُوبُ ۖ يَـٰبَنِىَّ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ ٱصْطَفَىٰ لَكُمُ ٱلدِّينَ فَلَا تَمُوتُنَّ إِلَّا وَأَنتُم مُّسْلِمُونَ
Wa waṣṣā bihā Ibrāhīmu banīhi wa Yaʿqūb…
And Abraham instructed his sons, and so did Jacob: “O my sons, Allah has chosen for you the religion, so do not die except as Muslims.”
133
أَمْ كُنتُمْ شُهَدَآءَ إِذْ حَضَرَ يَعْقُوبَ ٱلْمَوْتُ…
Am kuntum shuhadā’a idh ḥaḍara Yaʿqūbal-mawt…
Or were you witnesses when death approached Jacob…?
134
تِلْكَ أُمَّةٌۭ قَدْ خَلَتْ ۖ لَهَا مَا كَسَبَتْ…
Tilka ummatun qad khalat…
That was a nation that has passed away. It will have what it earned, and you will have what you earn.
135
وَقَالُوا۟ كُونُوا۟ هُودًا أَوْ نَصَـٰرَىٰ تَهْتَدُوا۟…
Wa qālū kūnū hūdan aw naṣārā tahtadū…
They say, “Be Jews or Christians, and you will be guided.” Say, “Rather the religion of Abraham…”
136
قُولُوا۟ ءَامَنَّا بِٱللَّهِ…
Qūlū āmannā billāh…
Say, “We believe in Allah and what has been revealed to us…”
137
فَإِنْ ءَامَنُوا۟ بِمِثْلِ مَآ ءَامَنتُم بِهِۦ فَقَدِ ٱهْتَدَوا۟…
Fa in āmanū…
So if they believe in the same as you believe, they are guided…
138
صِبْغَةَ ٱللَّهِ ۖ وَمَنْ أَحْسَنُ مِنَ ٱللَّهِ صِبْغَةًۭ…
Ṣibghata Allāh…
The color of Allah—and who is better than Allah in coloring?
139
قُلْ أَتُحَآجُّونَنَا فِى ٱللَّهِ…
Qul atuḥājjūnanā fīllāh…
Say, “Do you argue with us about Allah, while He is our Lord and your Lord?”
140
أَمْ تَقُولُونَ إِنَّ إِبْرَٰهِـۧمَ وَإِسْمَـٰعِيلَ…
Am taqūlūna inna Ibrāhīma wa Ismāʿīla…
Or do you say that Abraham, Ishmael, Isaac, Jacob… were Jews or Christians?
141
تِلْكَ أُمَّةٌۭ قَدْ خَلَتْ…
Tilka ummatun qad khalat…
That was a nation that has passed away…
142
سَيَقُولُ ٱلسُّفَهَآءُ مِنَ ٱلنَّاسِ…
Sayaqūlu as-sufahā’u…
The foolish among the people will say, “What has turned them from their qiblah…?”
143
وَكَذَٰلِكَ جَعَلْنَـٰكُمْ أُمَّةًۭ وَسَطًا…
Wa kadhālika jaʿalnākum ummatan wasaṭā…
Thus We made you a balanced nation…
144
قَدْ نَرَىٰ تَقَلُّبَ وَجْهِكَ فِى ٱلسَّمَآءِ…
Qad narā taqalluba wajhika fī as-samā’…
We have certainly seen the turning of your face toward the sky…
145
وَلَئِنْ أَتَيْتَ ٱلَّذِينَ أُوتُوا۟ ٱلْكِتَـٰبَ…
Wa la’in atayta lladhīna ūtū l-kitāb…
Even if you brought the People of the Book every sign, they would not follow your qiblah.
146
ٱلَّذِينَ ءَاتَيْنَـٰهُمُ ٱلْكِتَـٰبَ يَعْرِفُونَهُۥ…
Alladhīna ātaynāhumul-kitāb…
Those to whom We gave the Scripture recognize him as they recognize their own sons.
147
ٱلْحَقُّ مِن رَّبِّكَ فَلَا تَكُونَنَّ مِنَ ٱلْمُمْتَرِينَ
Al-ḥaqqu mir rabbik…
The truth is from your Lord, so never be among the doubters.
148
وَلِكُلٍّۢ وِجْهَةٌ هُوَ مُوَلِّيهَا…
Wa likullin wijhatun huwa muwallīhā…
For every nation is a direction toward which it faces. So race to all that is good.
149
وَمِنْ حَيْثُ خَرَجْتَ فَوَلِّ وَجْهَكَ شَطْرَ ٱلْمَسْجِدِ ٱلْحَرَامِ…
Wa min ḥaythu kharajta…
Wherever you go out, turn your face toward the Sacred Mosque.
150
وَمِنْ حَيْثُ خَرَجْتَ فَوَلِّ وَجْهَكَ شَطْرَ ٱلْمَسْجِدِ ٱلْحَرَامِ…
Wa min ḥaythu kharajta fawalli wajhaka shaṭral-masjidil-ḥarām…
Wherever you go out, turn your face toward the Sacred Mosque, so that people have no argument against you.
151
كَمَا أَرْسَلْنَا فِيكُمْ رَسُولًا مِّنكُمْ يَتْلُوا۟ عَلَيْكُمْ ءَايَٰتِنَا وَيُزَكِّيكُمْ وَيُعَلِّمُكُمُ ٱلْكِتَٰبَ وَٱلْحِكْمَةَ وَيُعَلِّمُكُم مَّا لَمْ تَكُونُوا۟ تَعْلَمُونَ
Kamā arsalnā fīkum rasūlan minkum yatlū ʿalaykum āyātinā wa yuzakkīkum wa yuʿallimukumu al-kitāba wal-ḥikmah wa yuʿallimukum mā lam takūnū taʿlamūn.
Just as We sent a messenger from among you, reciting Our signs to you, purifying you, teaching you the Book, wisdom, and what you did not know.
152
فَٱذْكُرُونِىٓ أَذْكُرْكُمْ وَٱشْكُرُوا۟ لِى وَلَا تَكْفُرُونِ
Fa-dhkurūnī adhkurkum washkurū lī wa lā takfurūn.
So remember Me, and I will remember you. Be grateful to Me and do not be ungrateful.
153
يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ ٱسْتَعِينُوا۟ بِٱلصَّبْرِ وَٱلصَّلَوٰةِ ۚ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ مَعَ ٱلصَّٰبِرِينَ
Yā ayyuhā alladhīna āmanū istaʿīnū biṣ-ṣabri waṣ-ṣalāh; inna Allāha maʿa aṣ-ṣābirīn.
O believers! Seek help through patience and prayer. Allah is with those who remain patient.
154
وَلَا تَقُولُوا۟ لِمَن يُقْتَلُ فِى سَبِيلِ ٱللَّهِ أَمْوَٰتٌۢ ۚ بَلْ أَحْيَآءٌۭ وَلَٰكِن لَّا تَشْعُرُونَ
Wa lā taqūlū liman yuqtalu fī sabīli Allāhi amwāt; bal aḥyā’, walākin lā tashʿurūn.
Do not say about those killed in Allah’s path that they are dead; rather, they are alive, though you do not perceive.
155
وَلَنَبْلُوَنَّكُم بِشَىْءٍۢ مِّنَ ٱلْخَوْفِ وَٱلْجُوعِ وَنَقْصٍۢ مِّنَ ٱلْأَمْوَٰلِ وَٱلْأَنفُسِ وَٱلثَّمَرَٰتِ ۗ وَبَشِّرِ ٱلصَّٰبِرِينَ
Wa lanabluwannakum bishay’in mina al-khawfi wal-jūʿi wa naqṣin mina al-amwāli wal-anfusi wath-thamarāt; wa bashshir aṣ-ṣābirīn.
We will surely test you with fear, hunger, and loss of wealth, lives, and crops. Give glad tidings to the patient.
156
ٱلَّذِينَ إِذَآ أَصَـٰبَتْهُم مُّصِيبَةٌۭ قَالُوٓا۟ إِنَّا لِلَّهِ وَإِنَّآ إِلَيْهِ رَٰجِعُونَ
Alladhīna idhā aṣābat-hum muṣībah qālū innā lillāhi wa innā ilayhi rājiʿūn.
Those who, when a trial strikes them, say: We belong to Allah, and to Him we will return.
157
أُو۟لَٰٓئِكَ عَلَيْهِمْ صَلَوَٰتٌۭ مِّن رَّبِّهِمْ وَرَحْمَةٌۭ ۖ وَأُو۟لَٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلْمُهْتَدُونَ
Ulā’ika ʿalayhim ṣalawātun min rabbihim wa raḥmah; wa ulā’ika humul-muhtadūn.
They are the ones who receive blessings and mercy from their Lord; they are the rightly guided.
158
إِنَّ ٱلصَّفَا وَٱلْمَرْوَةَ مِن شَعَآئِرِ ٱللَّهِ...
Inna aṣ-ṣafā wal-marwah min shaʿā’iri Allāh...
Indeed, Safa and Marwah are among the symbols of Allah. Whoever performs Hajj or Umrah has no blame in walking between them.
159
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ يَكْتُمُونَ مَآ أَنزَلْنَا...
Inna alladhīna yaktumūna mā anzalnā...
Those who conceal what We have revealed after We made it clear—Allah curses them, and the cursers curse them as well.
160
إِلَّا ٱلَّذِينَ تَابُوا۟ وَأَصْلَحُوا۟ وَبَيَّنُوا۟...
Illā alladhīna tābū wa aṣlaḥū wa bayyanū...
Except for those who repent, correct themselves, and openly declare the truth—those I will accept their repentance.
161
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ وَمَاتُوا۟ وَهُمْ كُفَّارٌ...
Inna alladhīna kafarū wa mātū wa hum kuffār...
Those who disbelieved and died while in disbelief—upon them is the curse of Allah, the angels, and all people.
162
خَٰلِدِينَ فِيهَا لَا يُخَفَّفُ عَنْهُمُ ٱلْعَذَابُ...
Khālidīna fīhā lā yukhaffafu ʿanhumul-ʿadhāb...
They will remain in it forever. The punishment will not be lightened, nor will they be given relief.
163
وَإِلَٰهُكُمْ إِلَٰهٌۭ وَٰحِدٌۭ...
Wa ilāhukum ilāhun wāḥid...
Your God is one God; none has the right to be worshipped except Him, the Most Compassionate, the Most Merciful.
164
إِنَّ فِى خَلْقِ ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ...
Inna fī khalqi as-samāwāti wal-arḍ...
Indeed, in the creation of the heavens and earth... are signs for people who use their intellect.
165
وَمِنَ ٱلنَّاسِ مَن يَتَّخِذُ مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ أَندَادًۭا...
Wa mina an-nāsi man yattakhidhu min dūni Allāhi andādā...
Yet among people are those who take others besides Allah as equals, loving them as they should love Allah.
166
إِذْ تَبَرَّأَ ٱلَّذِينَ ٱتُّبِعُوا۟ مِنَ ٱلَّذِينَ ٱتَّبَعُوا۟...
Idh tabarra’a alladhīna ittubiʿū min alladhīna ittabaʿū...
When the followed ones will disown those who followed them, and they will all see the punishment.
167
وَقَالَ ٱلَّذِينَ ٱتَّبَعُوا۟ لَوْ أَنَّ لَنَا كَرَّةًۭ فَنَتَبَرَّأَ مِنْهُمْ...
Wa qāla alladhīna ittabaʿū law anna lanā karratan...
Those who followed will say: If only we could return, we would disown them just as they disowned us.
168
يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلنَّاسُ كُلُوا۟ مِمَّا فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ حَلَٰلًۭا طَيِّبًۭا...
Yā ayyuhā an-nāsu kulū mimmā fī al-arḍi ḥalālan ṭayyibā...
O people! Eat from what is lawful and pure on the earth, and do not follow the footsteps of Satan.
169
إِنَّمَا يَأْمُرُكُم بِٱلسُّوٓءِ وَٱلْفَحْشَآءِ...
Innamā ya’murukum bis-sū’i wal-faḥshā’i...
He only commands you to commit evil and indecency, and to say about Allah what you do not know.
170
وَإِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمُ ٱتَّبِعُوا۟ مَآ أَنزَلَ ٱللَّهُ...
Wa idhā qīla lahumu ittabiʿū mā anzala Allāh...
And when it is said to them, "Follow what Allah has revealed," they say: “We follow what we found our forefathers upon.”
171
وَمَثَلُ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ كَمَثَلِ ٱلَّذِى يَنْعِقُ بِمَا لَا يَسْمَعُ...
Wa mathalu alladhīna kafarū kamathali alladhī yanʿiqu bimā lā yasmaʿu...
The example of the disbelievers is like one who shouts at an animal that hears only calls and cries. Deaf, dumb, and blind—they do not understand.
172
يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ كُلُوا۟ مِن طَيِّبَٰتِ مَا رَزَقْنَٰكُمْ...
Yā ayyuhā alladhīna āmanū kulū min ṭayyibāti mā razaqnākum...
O believers! Eat from the good things We have provided for you and be grateful to Allah if you truly worship Him.
173
إِنَّمَا حَرَّمَ عَلَيْكُمُ ٱلْمَيْتَةَ وَٱلدَّمَ وَلَحْمَ ٱلْخِنزِيرِ...
Innamā ḥarrama ʿalaykumu al-maytata wa ad-dama wa laḥma al-khinzīr...
He has only forbidden to you carrion, blood, pork, and what is dedicated to other than Allah. But if one is forced, without desire or excess, there is no sin on him.
174
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ يَكْتُمُونَ مَآ أَنزَلَ ٱللَّهُ مِنَ ٱلْكِتَٰبِ...
Inna alladhīna yaktumūna mā anzala Allāhu mina al-kitāb...
Those who conceal what Allah has revealed of the Book and exchange it for a small gain—they swallow nothing but fire into their bellies.
175
أُو۟لَٰٓئِكَ ٱلَّذِينَ ٱشْتَرَوُا۟ ٱلضَّلَٰلَةَ بِٱلْهُدَىٰ...
Ulā’ika alladhīna ishtaraw aḍ-ḍalālah bil-hudā...
They are the ones who purchased misguidance in exchange for guidance and punishment instead of forgiveness.
176
ذَٰلِكَ بِأَنَّ ٱللَّهَ نَزَّلَ ٱلْكِتَٰبَ بِٱلْحَقِّ...
Dhālika bi-anna Allāha nazzala al-kitāba bil-ḥaqq...
That is because Allah sent down the Book in truth. And those who differ over the Book are in deep opposition.
177
لَّيْسَ ٱلْبِرَّ أَن تُوَلُّوا۟ وُجُوهَكُمْ...
Laysa al-birra an tuwallū wujūhakum...
Righteousness is not turning your faces toward the east or west, but righteousness is in belief, charity, prayer, patience, and fulfilling promises.
178
يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ كُتِبَ عَلَيْكُمُ ٱلْقِصَاصُ...
Yā ayyuhā alladhīna āmanū kutiba ʿalaykumu al-qiṣāṣ...
O believers! Retribution is prescribed for you in cases of murder...
179
وَلَكُمْ فِى ٱلْقِصَاصِ حَيَوٰةٌۭ...
Wa lakum fī al-qiṣāṣi ḥayāh...
In retribution there is life for you, O people of understanding, so that you may be righteous.
180
كُتِبَ عَلَيْكُمْ إِذَا حَضَرَ أَحَدَكُمُ ٱلْمَوْتُ...
Kutiba ʿalaykum idhā ḥaḍara aḥadakumu al-mawt...
It is prescribed for you that when death approaches one of you, to make a bequest for parents and relatives with fairness.
181
فَمَن بَدَّلَهُۥ بَعْدَمَا سَمِعَهُۥ...
Faman baddalahū baʿdamā samiʿahū...
Whoever changes the will after hearing it—the sin is on those who change it. Allah is All-Hearing, All-Knowing.
182
فَمَنْ خَافَ مِن مُّوصٍۢ جَنَفًا أَوْ إِثْمًۭا...
Faman khāfa mim-mūṣin janafan aw ithmā...
But if someone fears partiality or sin from the testator and reconciles between the heirs—there is no sin on him.
183
يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ كُتِبَ عَلَيْكُمُ ٱلصِّيَامُ...
Yā ayyuhā alladhīna āmanū kutiba ʿalaykumu aṣ-ṣiyām...
O believers! Fasting is prescribed for you as it was for those before you, so that you may become righteous.
184
أَيَّامًۭا مَّعْدُودَٰتٍۢ...
Ayyāman maʿdūdāt...
A limited number of days. But whoever is ill or traveling—then an equal number from other days...
185
شَهْرُ رَمَضَانَ ٱلَّذِىٓ أُنزِلَ فِيهِ ٱلْقُرْءَانُ...
Shahru Ramaḍāna alladhī unzila fīhi al-Qur’ān...
The month of Ramadan is when the Qur’an was revealed, as guidance for humanity and clear proofs of guidance and criterion...
186
وَإِذَا سَأَلَكَ عِبَادِى عَنِّى فَإِنِّى قَرِيبٌ...
Wa idhā sa’alaka ʿibādī ʿannī fa innī qarīb...
When My servants ask you about Me—indeed I am near. I respond to the caller when he calls upon Me...
187
أُحِلَّ لَكُمْ لَيْلَةَ ٱلصِّيَامِ ٱلرَّفَثُ...
Uḥilla lakum laylata aṣ-ṣiyāmi ar-rafath...
It has been made lawful for you during the nights of fasting to be intimate with your wives—they are a garment for you and you are a garment for them...
188
وَلَا تَأْكُلُوٓا۟ أَمْوَٰلَكُم بَيْنَكُم بِٱلْبَٰطِلِ...
Wa lā ta’kulū amwālakum baynakum bil-bāṭil...
Do not consume one another’s wealth unjustly or use it to bribe authorities knowingly to consume a portion of wealth sinfully.
189
يَسْـَٔلُونَكَ عَنِ ٱلْأَهِلَّةِ...
Yasalūnaka ʿani al-ahillāh...
They ask you about the phases of the moon. Say: They are measurements of time for people and for Hajj...
190
وَقَٰتِلُوا۟ فِى سَبِيلِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلَّذِينَ يُقَٰتِلُونَكُمْ...
Wa qātilū fī sabīli Allāhi alladhīna yuqātilūnakum...
Fight in the cause of Allah those who fight you, but do not transgress. Allah does not love the transgressors.
191
وَٱقْتُلُوهُمْ حَيْثُ ثَقِفْتُمُوهُمْ...
Wa uqtulūhum ḥaythu thaqiftumūhum...
Kill them wherever you encounter them and expel them from where they expelled you. Persecution is worse than killing...
192
فَإِنِ ٱنتَهَوْا۟ فَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ غَفُورٌۭ رَّحِيمٌۭ
Fa-in intahaw fa inna Allāha ghafūrun raḥīm.
But if they cease, then Allah is Ever-Forgiving, Most Merciful.
193
وَقَٰتِلُوهُمْ حَتَّىٰ لَا تَكُونَ فِتْنَةٌۭ...
Wa qātilūhum ḥattā lā takūna fitnah...
Fight them until there is no persecution and religion is for Allah alone. But if they cease, then no hostility is permitted except against the wrongdoers.
194
ٱلشَّهْرُ ٱلْحَرَامُ بِٱلشَّهْرِ ٱلْحَرَامِ...
Ash-shahru al-ḥarāmu bish-shahri al-ḥarām...
A sacred month is for a sacred month, and violations are subject to retribution. Whoever attacks you—respond in the same manner, but fear Allah...
195
وَأَنفِقُوا۟ فِى سَبِيلِ ٱللَّهِ...
Wa anfiqū fī sabīli Allāh...
Spend in the cause of Allah and do not throw yourselves into destruction with your own hands...
196
وَأَتِمُّوا۟ ٱلْحَجَّ وَٱلْعُمَرَةَ لِلَّهِ...
Wa atimmū al-ḥajja wal-ʿumrata lillāh...
Complete the Hajj and Umrah for Allah. If you are prevented, then offer a sacrifice available with ease...
197
ٱلْحَجُّ أَشْهُرٌۭ مَّعْلُومَٰتٌۭ...
Al-ḥajju ashhurun maʿlūmāt...
Hajj is during well-known months. Whoever commits to Hajj in them—no intimacy, misconduct, or arguing is allowed during Hajj...
198
لَيْسَ عَلَيْكُمْ جُنَاحٌ أَن تَبْتَغُوا۟ فَضْلًۭا مِّن رَّبِّكُمْ...
Laysa ʿalaykum junāḥun an tabtaghū faḍlan min rabbikum...
There is no blame upon you to seek the bounty of your Lord during Hajj. When you depart from Arafat, remember Allah at the sacred place...
199
ثُمَّ أَفِيضُوا۟ مِنْ حَيْثُ أَفَاضَ ٱلنَّاسُ...
Thumma afīḍū min ḥaythu afāḍa an-nās...
Then depart from where all the people depart and ask Allah for forgiveness. Indeed, Allah is Forgiving, Merciful.
200
فَإِذَا قَضَيْتُمْ مَّنَاسِكَكُمْ...
Fa-idhā qaḍaytum manāsikakum...
When you have finished your rites, remember Allah as you used to remember your forefathers—or with even more intense remembrance...
201
وَمِنْهُم مَّن يَقُولُ رَبَّنَآ ءَاتِنَا فِى ٱلدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةًۭ...
Wa minhum man yaqūlu rabbana ātinā fid-dunyā ḥasanah...
And among them are those who say: “Our Lord, give us good in this world and good in the Hereafter, and protect us from the torment of the Fire.”
202
أُو۟لَٰٓئِكَ لَهُمْ نَصِيبٌۭ مِّمَّا كَسَبُوا۟...
Ulā’ika lahum naṣīb mimmā kasabū...
They will have a share of what they have earned. And Allah is swift in account.
203
وَٱذْكُرُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ فِىٓ أَيَّامٍۢ مَّعْدُودَٰتٍۢ...
Wadhkurū Allāha fī ayyāmin maʿdūdāt...
Remember Allah during the appointed days. Whoever hastens in two days—there is no sin on him; and whoever delays—there is no sin on him, for whoever is mindful of Allah...
204
وَمِنَ ٱلنَّاسِ مَن يُعْجِبُكَ قَوْلُهُۥ...
Wa mina an-nāsi man yuʿjibuka qawluh...
Among people is one whose speech impresses you in worldly life, and he calls Allah to witness what is in his heart, yet he is the fiercest in opposition.
205
وَإِذَا تَوَلَّىٰ سَعَىٰ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ لِيُفْسِدَ فِيهَا...
Wa idhā tawallā saʿā fil-arḍ li-yufsida fīhā...
And when he leaves, he strives throughout the land to spread corruption, destroying crops and livestock—and Allah does not love corruption.
206
وَإِذَا قِيلَ لَهُ ٱتَّقِ ٱللَّهَ أَخَذَتْهُ ٱلْعِزَّةُ بِٱلْإِثْمِ...
Wa idhā qīla lahu ittaqi Allāha akhadhat-hu al-ʿizzatu bil-ithm...
And when it is said to him: “Fear Allah,” pride drives him to sin. Hell is enough for him—a miserable resting place.
207
وَمِنَ ٱلنَّاسِ مَن يَشْرِى نَفْسَهُ ٱبْتِغَآءَ مَرْضَاتِ ٱللَّهِ...
Wa mina an-nāsi man yashrī nafsah ibtighā’a marḍāti Allāh...
And among people is one who sells himself seeking Allah’s pleasure. And Allah is Ever-Gracious to His servants.
208
يَٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ ٱدْخُلُوا۟ فِى ٱلسِّلْمِ كَآفَّةًۭ...
Yā ayyuhā alladhīna āmanū udkhulū fis-silmi kāffah...
O believers! Enter into Islam wholeheartedly and do not follow the footsteps of Satan—he is your clear enemy.
209
فَإِن زَلَلْتُم مِّنۢ بَعْدِ مَا جَآءَتْكُمُ ٱلْبَيِّنَٰتُ...
Fa-in zalaltum min baʿdi mā jā’atkumu al-bayyināt...
If you slip after the clear proofs have come to you—then know that Allah is Mighty, Wise.
210
هَلْ يَنظُرُونَ إِلَّآ أَن يَأْتِيَهُمُ ٱللَّهُ فِى ظُلَلٍۢ مِّنَ ٱلْغَمَٰمِ...
Hal yanẓurūna illā an yatīyahumu Allāhu fī ẓulalin min al-ghamām...
Are they waiting for Allah to come to them in the shade of clouds with the angels? The matter will then be settled, and to Allah all matters return.
211
سَلْ بَنِي إِسْرَائِيلَ كَمْ آتَيْنَاهُم مِّنْ آيَةٍۢ بَيِّنَةٍۢ...
Sal bani isrā’īla kam ātaynāhum min āyatin bayyinah...
Ask the Children of Israel how many clear signs We gave them. Whoever changes Allah’s favor after it comes to him—Allah is severe in punishment.
212
زُيِّنَ لِلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ ٱلْحَيَوٰةُ ٱلدُّنْيَا...
Zuyyina lilladhīna kafarū al-ḥayātu al-dunyā...
The life of this world is beautified for the disbelievers, and they mock the believers. But those who fear Allah will be above them on the Day of Resurrection. Allah provides to whom He wills without limit.
213
كَانَ ٱلنَّاسُ أُمَّةًۭ وَٰحِدَةًۭ فَبَعَثَ ٱللَّهُ ٱلنَّبِيِّۦنَ...
Kāna al-nāsu ummatan wāḥidatan faba‘atha Allāhu al-nabiyyīn...
Humanity was one community, then Allah sent prophets as bearers of good news and warning, and sent down the Book with truth to judge between people in their disputes.
214
أَمْ حَسِبْتُمْ أَن تَدْخُلُوا۟ ٱلْجَنَّةَ...
Am ḥasibtum an tadkhulū al-jannah...
Do you think you will enter Paradise without trials like those before you? Hardship and suffering touched them until the Messenger and believers said, “When will Allah’s help come?” Surely Allah’s help is near.
215
يَسْـَٔلُونَكَ مَاذَا يُنفِقُونَ...
Yas’alūnaka mādhā yunfiqūn...
They ask you what they should spend. Say: whatever good you spend is for parents, relatives, orphans, the poor, and travelers. Allah knows the good you do.
216
كُتِبَ عَلَيْكُمُ ٱلْقِتَالُ...
Kutiba ‘alaykumu al-qitāl...
Fighting is prescribed for you, though it is disliked by you. Perhaps you dislike something that is good for you, and like something that is bad for you. Allah knows, and you do not know.
217
يَسْـَٔلُونَكَ عَنِ ٱلشَّهْرِ ٱلْحَرَامِ...
Yas’alūnaka ‘ani al-shahri al-ḥarām...
They ask you about fighting in the sacred month. Say: fighting in it is a great sin, but blocking others from Allah’s path and the Sacred Mosque is even greater...
218
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَٱلَّذِينَ هَاجَرُوا۟...
Inna alladhīna āmanū wa alladhīna hājarū...
Indeed, those who believe, migrate, and strive in Allah’s way—they hope for Allah’s mercy. And Allah is Forgiving, Merciful.
219
يَسْـَٔلُونَكَ عَنِ ٱلْخَمْرِ وَٱلْمَيْسِرِ...
Yas’alūnaka ‘ani al-khamri wa al-maysir...
They ask you about wine and gambling. Say: there is great sin in both, and some benefit, but their sin is greater than their benefit...
220
فِى ٱلدُّنْيَا وَٱلْـَٔاخِرَةِ ۗ وَيَسْـَٔلُونَكَ عَنِ ٱلْيَتَـٰمَىٰ...
Fi al-dunyā wa al-ākhirah wa yas’alūnaka ‘ani al-yatāmā...
In this world and the next. They ask you about orphans. Say: improvement for them is best; and if you mix with them—they are your brothers. Allah knows the corrupt from the good...
221
وَلَا تَنكِحُوا۟ ٱلْمُشْرِكَـٰتِ...
Wa lā tankiḥū al-mushrikāt...
Do not marry idolatrous women until they believe. A believing slave woman is better than an idolatrous woman, even if she pleases you...
222
وَيَسْـَٔلُونَكَ عَنِ ٱلْمَحِيضِ...
Wa yas’alūnaka ‘ani al-maḥīḍ...
They ask you about menstruation. Say: it is discomfort, so keep away from them during menstruation and do not approach them until they are purified...
223
نِسَآؤُكُمْ حَرْثٌۭ لَّكُمْ...
Nisā’ukum ḥarthun lakum...
Your wives are a place of cultivation for you, so go to your cultivation as you wish, and send ahead for yourselves. And fear Allah and know you will meet Him...
224
وَلَا تَجْعَلُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ عُرْضَةًۭ لِّأَيْمَـٰنِكُمْ...
Wa lā taj‘alū Allāha ‘urḍatan li-aymānikum...
Do not make Allah an excuse in your oaths, blocking you from doing good, being righteous, and making peace among people. Allah is Hearing, Knowing.
225
لَّا يُؤَاخِذُكُمُ ٱللَّهُ بِاللَّغْوِ فِىٓ أَيْمَـٰنِكُمْ...
Lā yu’ākhidhukum Allāhu bi al-laghwi fī aymānikum...
Allah does not hold you accountable for unintentional oaths, but He holds you accountable for what your hearts commit. Allah is Forgiving, Forbearing.
226
لِّلَّذِينَ يُؤْلُونَ مِن نِّسَآئِهِمْ...
Lilladhīna yu’lūna min nisā’ihim...
For those who vow abstinence from their wives is a waiting of four months. If they return, Allah is Forgiving and Merciful.
227
وَإِنْ عَزَمُوا۟ ٱلطَّلَـٰقَ...
Wa in ‘azamū al-ṭalāq...
And if they decide on divorce—Allah is Hearing, Knowing.
228
وَٱلْمُطَلَّقَـٰتُ يَتَرَبَّصْنَ...
Wa al-muṭallaqātu yatarabbaṣna...
Divorced women shall wait for three cycles, and it is not lawful to hide what Allah has created in their wombs if they believe in Allah and the Last Day...
229
ٱلطَّلَـٰقُ مَرَّتَانِ...
Al-ṭalāqu marratān...
Divorce is twice. Then either keep her with kindness or release her with good treatment. It is not lawful to take back anything you gave them unless both fear they cannot maintain Allah’s limits...
230
فَإِن طَلَّقَهَا فَلَا تَحِلُّ لَهُۥ...
Fa in ṭallaqahā falā taḥillu lahu...
If he divorces her again, she is not lawful for him until she marries another husband. If he divorces her, there is no blame if they return to each other, if they think they can maintain Allah’s limits...
231
وَإِذَا طَلَّقْتُمُ النِّسَاءَ فَبَلَغْنَ أَجَلَهُنَّ فَأَمْسِكُوهُنَّ بِمَعْرُوفٍ أَوْ سَرِّحُوهُنَّ بِمَعْرُوفٍ...
Wa idhā ṭallaqtumu an-nisā’a fabalag̱na ajalahunna fa amsikūhunna bi-ma‘rūf aw sariḥūhunna bi-ma‘rūf...
When you divorce women and they have fulfilled their waiting period, either retain them in kindness or release them in kindness...
232
وَإِذَا طَلَّقْتُمُ النِّسَاءَ فَبَلَغْنَ أَجَلَهُنَّ فَلَا تَعْضُلُوهُنَّ...
Wa idhā ṭallaqtumu an-nisā’a fabalag̱na ajalahunna falā ta‘ḍulūhunna...
When you divorce women and they have completed their waiting period, do not prevent them from remarrying...
233
وَالْوَالِدَاتُ يُرْضِعْنَ أَوْلَادَهُنَّ حَوْلَيْنِ كَامِلَيْنِ...
Wal-wālidātu yurḍi‘na awlādahunna ḥawlayni kāmilayn...
Mothers may breastfeed their children for two complete years for whoever wishes to complete the nursing period...
234
وَالَّذِينَ يُتَوَفَّوْنَ مِنكُمْ وَيَذَرُونَ أَزْوَاجًا...
Wal-ladhīna yutawaffawna minkum wa yadharūna azwājan...
Those who die and leave behind wives should be waited for four months and ten days...
235
وَلَا جُنَاحَ عَلَيْكُمْ فِيمَا عَرَّضْتُم بِهِ مِنْ خِطْبَةِ النِّسَاءِ...
Wa lā junāḥa ‘alaykum fīmā ‘arraḍtum bihi min khiṭbati an-nisā’...
There is no blame upon you for hinting at a proposal to women or keeping it within yourselves...
236
لَّا جُنَاحَ عَلَيْكُمْ إِن طَلَّقْتُمُ النِّسَاءَ مَا لَمْ تَمَسُّوهُنَّ...
Lā junāḥa ‘alaykum in ṭallaqtumu an-nisā’a mā lam tamassūhunna...
There is no blame upon you if you divorce women before touching them or specifying a dowry...
237
وَإِن طَلَّقْتُمُوهُنَّ مِن قَبْلِ أَن تَمَسُّوهُنَّ وَقَدْ فَرَضْتُمْ لَهُنَّ فَرِيضَةً...
Wa in ṭallaqtumūhunna min qabli an tamassūhunna wa qad faraḍtum lahunna farīḍah...
And if you divorce them before touching them but after specifying a dowry, give them half of what you specified...
238
حَافِظُوا عَلَى الصَّلَوَاتِ وَالصَّلَاةِ الْوُسْطَىٰ...
Ḥāfiẓū ‘alaṣ-ṣalawāti waṣ-ṣalāti al-wusṭā...
Guard strictly the prayers, especially the middle prayer...
239
فَإِنْ خِفْتُمْ فَرِجَالًا أَوْ رُكْبَانًا...
Fa in khiftum farijālan aw rukbānan...
If you fear danger, pray while walking or riding...
240
وَالَّذِينَ يُتَوَفَّوْنَ مِنكُمْ وَيَذَرُونَ أَزْوَاجًا وَصِيَّةً...
Wal-ladhīna yutawaffawna minkum wa yadharūna azwājan waṣiyyatan...
Those among you who die and leave wives should make a bequest for them of one year’s maintenance without expulsion...
241
وَلِلْمُطَلَّقَاتِ مَتَاعٌۢ بِٱلْمَعْرُوفِ ۖ حَقًّا عَلَى ٱلْمُتَّقِينَ
Wa lil-muṭallaqāti matā‘un bil-ma‘rūfi ḥaqqan ‘alā al-muttaqīn.
And for divorced women is a provision according to what is acceptable—a duty upon the righteous.
242
كَذَٰلِكَ يُبَيِّنُ ٱللَّهُ لَكُمْ ءَايَٰتِهِۦ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَعْقِلُونَ
Kaḏālika yubayyinu Allāhu lakum āyātihi la‘allakum ta‘qilūn.
Thus does Allah make His verses clear to you so that you may understand.
243
أَلَمْ تَرَ إِلَى ٱلَّذِينَ خَرَجُوا۟ مِن دِيَٰرِهِمْ وَهُمْ أُلُوفٌ...
Alam tara ilā alladhīna kharajū min diyārihim wa hum ulūf...
Have you not considered those who left their homes in thousands, fearing death?...
244
وَقَٰتِلُوا۟ فِى سَبِيلِ ٱللَّهِ وَٱعْلَمُوٓا۟ أَنَّ ٱللَّهَ سَمِيعٌ عَلِيمٌ
Wa qātilū fī sabīli Allāhi wa‘lamū anna Allāha samī‘un ‘alīm.
Fight in the cause of Allah, and know that Allah is Hearing and Knowing.
245
مَّن ذَا ٱلَّذِى يُقْرِضُ ٱللَّهَ قَرْضًا حَسَنًۭا...
Man dhā alladhī yuqriḍu Allāha qarḍan ḥasanan...
Who is it that will lend Allah a good loan, so He may multiply it many times over?...
246
أَلَمْ تَرَ إِلَى ٱلْمَلَإِ مِنۢ بَنِىٓ إِسْرَٰٓءِيلَ...
Alam tara ilā al-mala’i min banī isrā’īl...
Have you not considered the chiefs of the Children of Israel after Moses?...
247
وَقَالَ لَهُمْ نَبِيُّهُمْ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ قَدْ بَعَثَ لَكُمْ طَالُوتَ...
Wa qāla lahum nabiyyuhum inna Allāha qad ba‘atha lakum ṭālūt...
Their prophet said to them: Allah has appointed Talut as king over you...
248
وَقَالَ لَهُمْ نَبِيُّهُمْ إِنَّ ءَايَةَ مُلْكِهِۦٓ...
Wa qāla lahum nabiyyuhum inna āyata mulkihi...
Their prophet said: The sign of his kingship is that the Ark will come to you...
249
فَلَمَّا فَصَلَ طَالُوتُ بِٱلْجُنُودِ...
Falammā faṣala ṭālūtu bil-junūd...
When Talut marched forth with the soldiers, he said: Allah will test you with a river...
250
وَلَمَّا بَرَزُوا۟ لِجَالُوتَ وَجُنُودِهِۦ...
Wa lammā barazū li-jālūta wa junūdih...
And when they confronted Goliath and his soldiers, they said: Our Lord, grant us patience...
251
فَهَزَمُوهُم بِإِذْنِ ٱللَّهِ ۖ وَقَتَلَ دَاوُۥدُ جَالُوتَ...
Fahazamūhum bi’idhni Allāh wa qatala dāwūdu jālūt...
They defeated them by Allah’s permission, and David killed Goliath...
252
تِلْكَ ءَايَٰتُ ٱللَّهِ نَتْلُوهَا عَلَيْكَ بِٱلْحَقِّ...
Tilka āyātu Allāhi natlūhā ‘alayka bil-ḥaqq...
These are the verses of Allah which We recite to you in truth...
253
تِلْكَ ٱلرُّسُلُ فَضَّلْنَا بَعْضَهُمْ عَلَىٰ بَعْضٍ...
Tilka ar-rusulu faḍḍalnā ba‘ḍahum ‘alā ba‘ḍ...
Those messengers—We favored some over others...
254
يَٰأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓا۟ أَنفِقُوا۟ مِمَّا رَزَقْنَٰكُم...
Yā ayyuhā alladhīna āmanū anfiqū mimmā razaqnākum...
O believers! Spend from what We have provided you before a Day comes with no trade or friendship...
255
ٱللَّهُ لَآ إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ ٱلْحَىُّ ٱلْقَيُّومُ...
Allāhu lā ilāha illā huwa al-ḥayyu al-qayyūm...
Allah! There is no deity except Him, the Ever-Living, the Sustainer of all existence...
256
لَآ إِكْرَاهَ فِى ٱلدِّينِ...
Lā ikrāha fid-dīn...
There is no compulsion in religion. Truth has become distinct from falsehood...
257
ٱللَّهُ وَلِىُّ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟...
Allāhu waliyyu alladhīna āmanū...
Allah is the Protector of those who believe...
258
أَلَمْ تَرَ إِلَى ٱلَّذِى حَآجَّ إِبْرَٰهِيمَ فِى رَبِّهِ...
Alam tara ilā alladhī ḥājjā ibrāhīma fī rabbih...
Have you not considered him who argued with Abraham about his Lord?...
259
أَوْ كَٱلَّذِى مَرَّ عَلَىٰ قَرْيَةٍۢ وَهِىَ خَاوِيَةٌ عَلَىٰ عُرُوشِهَا...
Aw kalladhī marra ‘alā qaryatin wa hiya khāwiyatun ‘alā ‘urūshihā...
Or like him who passed by a town that had collapsed on its roofs...
260
وَإِذْ قَالَ إِبْرَٰهِيمُ رَبِّ أَرِنِى كَيْفَ تُحْىِ ٱلْمَوْتَىٰ...
Wa idh qāla ibrāhīmu rabbi arini kayfa tuḥyī al-mawtā...
And when Abraham said: My Lord, show me how You give life to the dead...
261
مَّثَلُ ٱلَّذِينَ يُنفِقُونَ أَمْوَٰلَهُمْ فِى سَبِيلِ ٱللَّهِ كَمَثَلِ حَبَّةٍ...
Mathalu alladhīna yunfiqūna amwālahum fī sabīli Allāh kamathali ḥabbah...
The example of those who spend their wealth in the cause of Allah is like a seed that grows seven ears...
262
ٱلَّذِينَ يُنفِقُونَ أَمْوَٰلَهُمْ فِى سَبِيلِ ٱللَّهِ...
Alladhīna yunfiqūna amwālahum fī sabīli Allāh...
Those who spend their wealth in the cause of Allah and do not follow their spending with reminders or injury...
263
قَوْلٌۭ مَّعْرُوفٌۭ وَمَغْفِرَةٌ خَيْرٌۭ مِّن صَدَقَةٍۢ يَتْبَعُهَآ أَذًى...
Qawlun ma‘rūfun wa maghfiratun khayrun min ṣadaqatin yatba‘uhā adhā...
A kind word and forgiveness are better than charity followed by injury...
264
يَٰأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ لَا تُبْطِلُوا۟ صَدَقَٰتِكُم بِٱلْمَنِّ وَٱلْأَذَىٰ...
Yā ayyuhā alladhīna āmanū lā tubṭilū ṣadaqātikum bil-mann wal-aḏā...
O believers! Do not nullify your charities with reminders or injury...
265
وَمَثَلُ ٱلَّذِينَ يُنفِقُونَ أَمْوَٰلَهُمْ...
Wa mathalu alladhīna yunfiqūna amwālahum...
And the example of those who spend their wealth seeking Allah’s pleasure is like a garden on a high ground...
266
أَيَوَدُّ أَحَدُكُمْ أَن تَكُونَ لَهُۥ جَنَّةٌۭ مِّن نَّخِيلٍۢ وَأَعْنَٰبٍۢ...
Ayawaddu aḥadukum an takūna lahu jannatun min nakhīlin wa a‘nāb...
Would any of you like to have a garden of palm trees and vines... then it is struck by a whirlwind with fire?
267
يَٰأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓا۟ أَنفِقُوا۟ مِن طَيِّبَٰتِ مَا كَسَبْتُمْ...
Yā ayyuhā alladhīna āmanū anfiqū min ṭayyibāti mā kasabtum...
O believers! Spend from the good things you have earned...
268
ٱلشَّيْطَٰنُ يَعِدُكُمُ ٱلْفَقْرَ وَيَأْمُرُكُم بِٱلْفَحْشَآءِ...
Ash-shayṭānu ya‘idukum al-faqra wa ya’murukum bil-faḥshā’...
Satan threatens you with poverty and commands you with immorality...
269
يُؤْتِى ٱلْحِكْمَةَ مَن يَشَآءُ...
Yu’tī al-ḥikmata mayyashā’...
He grants wisdom to whom He wills, and whoever is given wisdom has been given much good...
270
وَمَآ أَنفَقْتُم مِّن نَّفَقَةٍ أَوْ نَذَرْتُم مِّن نَّذْرٍ...
Wa mā anfaqtum min nafaqatin aw nadhartum min nadhar...
Whatever you spend or vow—indeed Allah knows it...
271
إِن تُبْدُوا۟ ٱلصَّدَقَٰتِ فَنِعِمَّا هِىَ...
In tubdū ṣ-ṣadaqāti fani‘immā hiya...
If you disclose your charities—that is good; but if you conceal them and give to the poor, it is better for you...
272
لَّيْسَ عَلَيْكَ هُدَىٰهُمْ...
Laysa ‘alayka hudāhum...
Guiding them is not your responsibility, but Allah guides whom He wills...
273
لِلْفُقَرَآءِ ٱلَّذِينَ أُحْصِرُوا۟ فِى سَبِيلِ ٱللَّهِ...
Lil-fuqarā’ alladhīna uḥṣirū fī sabīli Allāh...
Charity is for the poor who are restricted for the cause of Allah...
274
ٱلَّذِينَ يُنفِقُونَ أَمْوَٰلَهُم بِٱلَّيْلِ وَٱلنَّهَارِ سِرًّۭا وَعَلَانِيَةًۭ...
Alladhīna yunfiqūna amwālahum bil-layli wan-nahār sirran wa ‘alāniyah...
Those who spend their wealth night and day, secretly and publicly—they will have their reward with their Lord...
275
ٱلَّذِينَ يَأْكُلُونَ ٱلرِّبَوٰا...
Alladhīna ya’kulūna ar-ribā...
Those who consume interest will not stand except like one beaten by Satan into insanity...
276
يَمْحَقُ ٱللَّهُ ٱلرِّبَوٰا وَيُرْبِى ٱلصَّدَقَٰتِ...
Yamḥaqu Allāhu ar-ribā wa yurbi ṣ-ṣadaqāt...
Allah destroys interest and gives increase to charities...
277
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَعَمِلُوا۟ ٱلصَّٰلِحَٰتِ...
Inna alladhīna āmanū wa ‘amilūṣ-ṣāliḥāt...
Indeed, those who believe and do righteous deeds and establish prayer and give charity—they will have their reward from their Lord...
278
يَٰأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ ٱتَّقُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ وَذَرُوا۟ مَا بَقِىَ مِنَ ٱلرِّبَوٰا...
Yā ayyuhā alladhīna āmanū ittaqū Allāha wa dharū mā baqiya mina r-ribā...
O believers! Fear Allah and give up what remains of interest, if you are truly believers...
279
فَإِن لَّمْ تَفْعَلُوا۟ فَأْذَنُوا۟ بِحَرْبٍۢ مِّنَ ٱللَّهِ وَرَسُولِهِۦ...
Fa in lam taf‘alū fa’dhanū biḥarbin min Allāhi wa rasūlih...
If you do not give it up, then be informed of war from Allah and His Messenger...
280
وَإِن كَانَ ذُو عُسْرَةٍۢ فَنَظِرَةٌ إِلَىٰ مَيْسَرَةٍۢ...
Wa in kāna dhū ‘usratin fanaẓiratun ilā maysarah...
And if someone is in hardship, then let there be postponement until ease...
281
وَٱتَّقُوا۟ يَوْمًۭا تُرْجَعُونَ فِيهِ إِلَى ٱللَّهِ...
Wattaqū yawman turja‘ūna fīhi ilā Allāh...
And fear the Day when you will be returned to Allah...
282
يَٰأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓا۟ إِذَا تَدَايَنتُم بِدَيْنٍ...
Yā ayyuhā alladhīna āmanū idhā tadāyantum bidaynin...
O believers! When you contract a loan for a fixed period, write it down...
283
وَإِن كُنتُمْ عَلَىٰ سَفَرٍۢ وَلَمْ تَجِدُوا۟ كَاتِبًۭا...
Wa in kuntum ‘alā safarin walam tajidū kātiban...
And if you are on a journey and cannot find a scribe, then a security deposit should be taken...
284
لِّلَّهِ مَا فِى ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَمَا فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ...
Lillāhi mā fis-samāwāti wa mā fil-arḍ...
To Allah belongs whatever is in the heavens and whatever is on the earth...
285
ءَامَنَ ٱلرَّسُولُ بِمَآ أُنزِلَ إِلَيْهِ مِن رَّبِّهِۦ...
Āmana ar-rasūlu bimā unzila ilayhi min rabbih...
The Messenger has believed in what was revealed to him from his Lord, and so have the believers...
286
لَا يُكَلِّفُ ٱللَّهُ نَفْسًا إِلَّا وُسْعَهَا...
Lā yukallifu Allāhu nafsan illā wus‘ahā...
Allah does not burden a soul beyond what it can bear...
