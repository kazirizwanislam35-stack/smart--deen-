Hadith
Hadith 1
إِنَّمَا الْأَعْمَالُ بِالنِّيَّاتِ
Actions are judged by intentions.
Hadith 2
الدِّينُ النَّصِيحَةُ
Religion is sincere advice.
Hadith 3
لَا يُؤْمِنُ أَحَدُكُمْ حَتَّى يُحِبَّ لِأَخِيهِ مَا يُحِبُّ لِنَفْسِهِ
None of you truly believes until he loves for his brother what he loves for himself.
Hadith 4
الْمُسْلِمُ مَنْ سَلِمَ الْمُسْلِمُونَ مِنْ لِسَانِهِ وَيَدِهِ
A Muslim is the one from whose tongue and hands others are safe.
Hadith 5
مَنْ صَمَتَ نَجَا
Whoever remains silent is saved.
Hadith 6
طَهُورُ شَطْرُ الإِيمَانِ
Purity is half of faith.
Hadith 7
الْجَنَّةُ تَحْتَ أَقْدَامِ الْأُمَّهَاتِ
Paradise lies under the feet of mothers.
Hadith 8
الْمَرْءُ مَعَ مَنْ أَحَبَّ
A person will be with whom he loves.
Hadith 9
مَنْ لَا يَرْحَمْ لَا يُرْحَمْ
Whoever does not show mercy will not receive mercy.
Hadith 10
خَيْرُكُمْ خَيْرُكُمْ لِأَهْلِهِ
The best of you is the one who is best to his family.
Hadith 11
الحَيَاءُ خَيْرٌ كُلُّهُ
Modesty brings nothing but good.
Hadith 12
مَنْ تَوَاضَعَ لِلَّهِ رَفَعَهُ
Whoever humbles himself for Allah, He raises him.
Hadith 13
الدُّعَاءُ مُخُّ الْعِبَادَةِ
Dua is the essence of worship.
Hadith 14
الْجَنَّةُ قَرِيبَةٌ مِنْ أَحَدِكُمْ
Paradise is closer to you than your shoelace.
Hadith 15
السَّخَاءُ شَجَرَةٌ مِنَ الْجَنَّةِ
Generosity is a tree of Paradise.
Hadith 16
مَنْ غَشَّ فَلَيْسَ مِنَّا
Whoever cheats is not one of us.
Hadith 17
إِبْتِسَامُكَ فِي وَجْهِ أَخِيكَ صَدَقَةٌ
Your smile for your brother is charity.
Hadith 18
الدُّنْيَا سِجْنُ الْمُؤْمِنِ
The world is a prison for the believer.
Hadith 19
المُؤْمِنُ مِرْآةُ أَخِيهِ
A believer is the mirror of another believer.
Hadith 20
الرِّضَا بِاللَّهِ رَبًّا
Contentment is true richness.
