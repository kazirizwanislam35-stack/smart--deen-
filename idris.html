Prophet Idris (A.S) — Full Story

Prophet Idris (A.S), peace be upon him, stands among the earliest prophets in human history, following Prophet Adam (A.S) and Prophet Sheeth (A.S). He is honored in the Qur’an as a man of truth and patience, and his life serves as a remarkable example of devotion, wisdom, and steadfastness. Idris (A.S) is believed to be the first person to write with a pen, sew clothes, and study astronomy — truly a pioneer in human civilization.

Allah elevated him to a high station, a unique honor mentioned in the Qur’an. His ascension reflects his extraordinary faith and obedience. Idris (A.S) spent his life calling people toward goodness, teaching them divine knowledge, and encouraging righteous living.

Wisdom, Knowledge, and Revelation

Idris (A.S) received divine scriptures and was known for his deep reflection on the mysteries of creation. He was the first to understand the movements of stars and the measurement of time. His intellect and prophetic insight helped guide early communities toward structure, discipline, and moral conduct.

Ascension to the Heavens

One of the most extraordinary aspects of his life is his ascension. Allah says:

“And We raised him to a high station.”

Scholars interpret this as a spiritual elevation, a physical ascension, or both. This honor symbolizes his closeness to Allah and his elevated spiritual rank among prophets.

His Legacy for Believers

Prophet Idris (A.S) left behind a legacy of patience, faith, reflection, and the pursuit of knowledge. His example teaches believers to seek wisdom, remain steadfast in trials, and live with discipline and sincerity.

Reflection:

Consider how patience strengthens your faith. Think of a moment when you remained calm during difficulty — how did it bring you closer to Allah? Write a short reflection on how you can adopt Idris’s qualities of wisdom, discipline, and gratitude.
