Al-Imran
Revealed: Madinah • Verses: 200 • Meaning: The Family of Imran
1
الم
Alif-Lām-Mīm
Letter combination — a Divine sign.
2
اللَّهُ لَا إِلَـٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ الْحَيُّ الْقَيُّومُ
Allāhu lā ilāha illā huwa al-Ḥayyul-Qayyūm
Allah — none has the right to be worshiped but He, Ever-Living.
3
نَزَّلَ عَلَيْكَ الْكِتَابَ بِالْحَقِّ مُصَدِّقاً لِمَا بَيْنَ يَدَيْهِ وَأَنْزَلَ التَّوْرَاةَ...
Nazzala 'alayka al-kitāba bil-ḥaqqi muṣaddiqan...
He sent the Book to confirm what came before and brought guidance.
4
مِنَ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ وَالْمُهَاجِرِينَ وَالْأَنصَارِ...
Min al-mu'minīna wa al-muhājirīna wa al-anṣār...
Those who believe, migrate and strive for Allah — a high status.
5
وَأُولَـٰئِكَ هُمُ الصَّادِقُونَ
Wa ulā'ika humu aṣ-ṣādiqūn
They are the truthful ones.
6
إِنَّمَا الْمُنَافِقُونَ يُخَادِعُونَ اللَّهَ وَهُوَ خَادِعُهُمْ
Innamal-munāfiqūna yukādhirūna Allāha wa huwa khādi'uhum
The hypocrites attempt to deceive Allah — but He knows them.
7
هُوَ الَّذِي أَنْزَلَ عَلَيْكَ الْكِتَابَ مِنْهُ آيَاتٌ مُحْكَمَاتٌ...
Huwa alladhī anzala 'alayka al-kitāba minhu āyātun muḥkamāt...
He revealed certain verses clear in meaning — the core of the Book.
8
رَبَّنَا لَا تُزِغْ قُلُوبَنَا بَعْدَ إِذْ هَدَيْتَنَا...
Rabbana lā tuzigh qulūbanā ba'da idh hadaytanā...
Our Lord — do not let our hearts deviate after You guided us.
9
رَبَّنَا إِنَّكَ جَامِعُ النَّاسِ لِيَوْمٍ لَا رَيْبَ فِيهِ...
Rabbana innaka jāmi'u an-nās li-yawmin lā rayba fīh...
Our Lord — You will gather people for a Day about which there is no doubt.
10
إِنَّ اللَّهَ لَا يَخْفَىٰ عَلَيْهِ شَيْءٌ فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ
Inna Allāha lā yakhfā 'alayhi shay'un fid-samāwāti wal-arḍ
Nothing in the heavens or earth is hidden from Allah.
11
إِنَّ اللَّهَ سَيُدْخِلُ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ...
Inna Allāha sayudkhilu alladhīna āmanū wa 'amilū aṣ-ṣāliḥāti...
Allah will admit those who believed and did good into Gardens.
12
وَمَا كَانَ لِنَبِيٍّ أَنْ يَأْتِيَهُ اَللَّهُ بِآيَةٍ إِلَّا بِإِذْنِهِ...
Wa mā kāna li nabiyyin an ya'tiyahu Allāhu bi-āyatin illā bi-idhnih...
No prophet received a sign except by Allah’s permission.
13
قَدْ كَفَرَ الَّذِينَ قَالُوا إِنَّ اللَّهَ ثَالِثٌ مِثْلُهُمْ...
Qad kafara alladhīna qālū inna Allāha thālithun mithluhum...
Those who say God has a son — their claim is false.
14
قُلْ إِنَّمَا أَدْعُو رَبِّي وَلَا أُشْرِكُ بِهِ شَيْئًا
Qul innamā ad'ū rabbī wa lā ushrik bihi shay'an
Say: I call upon my Lord and do not associate partners with Him.
15
قُلْ يَـٰأَهْلَ الْكِتَابِ تَعَالَوْا إِلَىٰ كَلِمَةٍ سَوَاءٍ...
Qul yā ahl al-kitābi ta'ālū ilā kalimatin sawā'in...
O People of the Book — come to a common word: worship none but Allah.
16
قَدْ جَاءَكُمْ بِهِ نَبَأٌ مِّنْ أَمْرِ اللَّهِ...
Qad jā'akum bihi nabā'un min amri Allāh...
A clear message has come to you from your Lord.
17
إِنَّمَا الْمُؤْمِنُونَ وَالْمُؤْمِنَاتُ وَالْقَانِتُونَ...
Innamal-mu'minūna wal-mu'minātu wal-qānitūn...
The believers — men and women — who humble themselves and do good will be rewarded.
18
إِنَّ اللَّهَ مَعَنَا وَهُوَ أَكْثَرُ شَهِيدًا
Inna Allāha ma'ana wa huwa aktharu shahīdān
Allah is with us and is the best Witness.
19
وَيَوْمَ نَحْشُرُهُمْ كَأَنْ لَمْ يَلِدُوا شَيْئًا...
Wa yawma naḥshuruhum ka'an lam yalidū shay'an...
On the Day We will resurrect them as if they had not existed before.
20
قُلْ أَرَأَيْتُمْ إِنْ أَهْلَكَنِيَ اللَّـهُ وَالَّذِينَ مَعِي...
Qul ara'aytum in ahlakani Allāhu wa alladhīna ma'ī...
Say: What if Allah destroys me and those with me — will you be patient?
21
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ يَكْفُرُونَ بِـَٔايَٰتِ ٱللَّهِ وَيَقْتُلُونَ ٱلنَّبِيِّۦنَ بِغَيْرِ حَقٍّ وَيَقْتُلُونَ ٱلَّذِينَ يَأْمُرُونَ بِٱلْقِسْطِ مِنَ ٱلنَّاسِ فَبَشِّرْهُم بِعَذَابٍ أَلِيمٍ
Inna alladhīna yakfurūna bi-āyāti Allāhi wa yaqtulūna an-nabiyyīna bighayri ḥaqqin wa yaqtulūna alladhīna yaʾmurūna bil-qisṭi mina an-nāsi fabashshirhum biʿadhābin alīm.
Indeed, those who deny Allah’s revelations and kill the prophets unjustly and kill those who call for justice — give them news of a painful punishment.
22
أُو۟لَٰٓئِكَ ٱلَّذِينَ حَبِطَتْ أَعْمَٰلُهُمْ فِى ٱلدُّنْيَا وَٱلْـَٔاخِرَةِ ۖ وَمَا لَهُم مِّن نَّٰصِرِينَ
Ulāʾika alladhīna ḥabiṭat aʿmāluhum fī ad-dunyā wal-ākhirah wa mā lahum min nāṣirīn.
They are the ones whose deeds are ruined in this world and the Hereafter, and they will have no helpers.
23
أَلَمْ تَرَ إِلَى ٱلَّذِينَ أُوتُوا۟ نَصِيبًا مِّنَ ٱلْكِتَٰبِ يُدْعَوْنَ إِلَىٰ كِتَٰبِ ٱللَّهِ...
Alam tara ila alladhīna ūtū naṣīban mina al-kitābi yudʿawna ilā kitābi Allāhi...
Have you not seen those who were given a portion of the Book being invited to the Book of Allah to judge between them?
24
ذَٰلِكَ بِأَنَّهُمْ قَالُوا۟ لَن تَمَسَّنَا ٱلنَّارُ إِلَّآ أَيَّامًۭا مَّعْدُودَٰتٍۢ...
Dhālika bi-annahum qālū lan tamassanā an-nāru illā ayyāman maʿdūdāt...
This is because they say, “The Fire will not touch us except for a few days.”
25
فَكَيْفَ إِذَا جَمَعْنَٰهُمْ لِيَوْمٍۢ لَّا رَيْبَ فِيهِ...
Fakayfa idhā jamaʿnāhum li-yawmin lā rayba fīh...
How will it be when We gather them for a Day about which there is no doubt?
26
قُلِ ٱللَّهُمَّ مَٰلِكَ ٱلْمُلْكِ...
Qul Allāhumma mālika al-mulki...
Say, “O Allah, Owner of all sovereignty, You give kingship to whom You will…”
27
تُولِجُ ٱلَّيْلَ فِى ٱلنَّهَارِ وَتُولِجُ ٱلنَّهَارَ فِى ٱلَّيْلِ...
Tūlij al-layla fī an-nahāri wa tūlij an-nahāra fī al-layl...
You merge the night into the day and the day into the night…”
28
لَّا يَتَّخِذِ ٱلْمُؤْمِنُونَ ٱلْكَٰفِرِينَ أَوْلِيَآءَ...
Lā yattakhidh al-muʾminūna al-kāfirīna awliyāʾ...
Believers should not take disbelievers as allies instead of believers…”
29
قُلْ إِن تُخْفُوا۟ مَا فِى صُدُورِكُمْ أَوْ تُبْدُوهُ يَعْلَمْهُ ٱللَّهُ...
Qul in tukhfū mā fī ṣudūrikum aw tubdūhu yaʿlamhu Allāh...
Say, “Whether you hide what is in your hearts or reveal it, Allah knows it.”
30
يَوْمَ تَجِدُ كُلُّ نَفْسٍۢ مَّا عَمِلَتْ مِنْ خَيْرٍۢ مُّحْضَرًۭا...
Yawma tajidu kullu nafsin mā ʿamilat min khayrin muḥḍarā...
The Day every soul will find the good it has done present before it…”
31
قُلْ إِن كُنتُمْ تُحِبُّونَ ٱللَّهَ فَٱتَّبِعُونِى يُحْبِبْكُمُ ٱللَّهُ...
Qul in kuntum tuḥibbūna Allāha fattabiʿūnī yuḥbibkumu Allāh...
Say, “If you love Allah, follow me; Allah will love you…”
32
قُلْ أَطِيعُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ وَٱلرَّسُولَ...
Qul aṭīʿū Allāha war-rasūl...
Say, “Obey Allah and the Messenger.”
33
إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ ٱصْطَفَىٰٓ ءَادَمَ وَنُوحًۭا وَءَالَ إِبْرَٰهِيمَ وَءَالَ عِمْرَٰنَ عَلَى ٱلْعَٰلَمِينَ
Inna Allāha iṣṭafā Ādama wa Nūḥan wa āla Ibrāhīma wa āla ʿImrān ʿalā al-ʿālamīn.
Indeed, Allah chose Adam, Noah, the family of Abraham, and the family of Imran over all people.
34
ذُرِّيَّةًۢ بَعْضُهَا مِنۢ بَعْضٍۢ ۗ وَٱللَّهُ سَمِيعٌ عَلِيمٌۭ
Dhurrīyatan baʿḍuhā min baʿḍ; wa Allāhu samīʿun ʿalīm.
A descendant of one another. And Allah is All-Hearing, All-Knowing.
35
إِذْ قَالَتِ ٱمْرَأَتُ عِمْرَٰنَ رَبِّ إِنِّى نَذَرْتُ لَكَ مَا فِى بَطْنِى...
Idh qālati imraʾatu ʿImrān rabbi innī nadhartu laka mā fī baṭnī...
When the wife of Imran said, “My Lord, I dedicate what is in my womb entirely to You…”
36
فَلَمَّا وَضَعَتْهَا قَالَتْ رَبِّ إِنِّى وَضَعْتُهَآ أُنثَىٰ...
Falammā waḍaʿathā qālat rabbi innī waḍaʿtuhā unthā...
When she delivered her, she said, “My Lord, I have delivered a female…”
37
فَتَقَبَّلَهَا رَبُّهَا بِقَبُولٍۢ حَسَنٍۢ...
Fataqabbalahā rabbuhā biqabūlin ḥasan...
So her Lord accepted her with gracious acceptance…”
38
هُنَالِكَ دَعَا زَكَرِيَّا رَبَّهُۥ...
Hunālika daʿā Zakariyyā rabbah...
There Zakariya prayed to his Lord…”
39
فَنَادَتْهُ ٱلْمَلَـٰٓئِكَةُ وَهُوَ قَآئِمٌۭ يُصَلِّى...
Fanādathu al-malāʾikatu wa huwa qāʾim yuṣallī...
Then the angels called to him while he stood praying…”
40
قَالَ رَبِّ أَنَّىٰ يَكُونُ لِى غُلَـٰمٌۭ وَقَدْ بَلَغَنِىَ ٱلْكِبَرُ...
Qāla rabbi annā yakūnu lī ghulāmun wa qad balagha-niya al-kibar...
He said, “My Lord, how will I have a boy when old age has overtaken me?”
41
قَالَ رَبِّ اجْعَل لِّيٓ ءَايَةًۭ ۚ قَالَ ءَايَتُكَ أَلَّا تُكَلِّمَ ٱلنَّاسَ ثَلَـٰثَةَ أَيَّامٍ إِلَّا رَمْزًۭا ۗ وَٱذْكُر رَّبَّكَ كَثِيرًۭا وَسَبِّحْ بِٱلْعَشِىِّ وَٱلْإِبْكَـٰرِ
Qāla rabbi ij‘al lī āyatan; qāla āyatuka allā tukalliman-nāsa thalāthata ayyāmin illā ramzā...
He said, “My Lord, make for me a sign.” He said, “Your sign is that you will not speak to people for three days except by gesture...”
42
وَإِذْ قَالَتِ ٱلْمَلَـٰٓئِكَةُ يَـٰمَرْيَمُ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ ٱصْطَفَىٰكِ وَطَهَّرَكِ وَٱصْطَفَىٰكِ عَلَىٰ نِسَآءِ ٱلْعَـٰلَمِينَ
Wa idh qālati al-malā’ikatu yā Maryam...
And when the angels said: O Mary, indeed Allah has chosen you and purified you and chosen you above the women of the worlds.
43
يَـٰمَرْيَمُ ٱقْنُتِى لِرَبِّكِ وَٱسْجُدِى وَٱرْكَعِى مَعَ ٱلرَّ‌ٰكِعِينَ
Yā Maryamu oqnutī li-rabbiki wasjudī warka‘ī ma‘ar-rāki‘īn.
O Mary, be devoutly obedient to your Lord, and prostrate and bow with those who bow.
44
ذَ‌ٰلِكَ مِنْ أَنۢبَآءِ ٱلْغَيْبِ نُوحِيهِ إِلَيْكَ...
Dhālika min anba’il-ghaybi nūḥīhi ilayk...
That is from the news of the unseen which We reveal to you...
45
إِذْ قَالَتِ ٱلْمَلَـٰٓئِكَةُ يَـٰمَرْيَمُ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ يُبَشِّرُكِ...
Idh qālati al-malā’ikatu yā Maryam inna Allāha yubashshiruki...
When the angels said: O Mary, indeed Allah gives you glad tidings of a word from Him, his name will be the Messiah, Jesus son of Mary...
46
وَيُكَلِّمُ ٱلنَّاسَ فِى ٱلْمَهْدِ وَكَهْلًۭا وَمِنَ ٱلصَّـٰلِحِينَ
Wa yukallimun-nāsa fil-mahdi wa kahlan wa minaṣ-ṣāliḥīn.
And he will speak to the people in the cradle and in maturity and he will be of the righteous.
47
قَالَتْ رَبِّ أَنَّىٰ يَكُونُ لِى وَلَدٌ وَلَمْ يَمْسَسْنِى بَشَرٌ...
Qālat rabbi annā yakūnu lī waladun wa lam yamsasnī bashar...
She said: “My Lord, how can I have a child when no man has touched me?”...
48
وَيُعَلِّمُهُ ٱلْكِتَـٰبَ وَٱلْحِكْمَةَ وَٱلتَّوْرَىٰةَ وَٱلْإِنجِيلَ
Wa yu‘allimuhul-kitāba wal-ḥikmata wat-tawrāta wal-injīl.
And He will teach him the Book and wisdom and the Torah and the Gospel.
49
وَرَسُولًا إِلَىٰ بَنِىٓ إِسْرَ‌ٰٓءِيلَ...
Wa rasūlan ilā banī Isrā’īl...
And as a messenger to the Children of Israel...
50
وَمُصَدِّقًا لِّمَا بَيْنَ يَدَيَّ مِنَ ٱلتَّوْرَىٰةِ وَلِأُحِلَّ لَكُم بَعْضَ ٱلَّذِى حُرِّمَ عَلَيْكُمْ ۚ وَجِئْتُكُم بِـَٔايَةٍ مِّن رَّبِّكُمْ فَٱتَّقُوا ٱللَّهَ وَأَطِيعُونِ
Wa muṣaddiqan limā bayna yadayya mina at-tawrāti wa li’uḥilla lakum ba‘ḍa alladhī ḥurrima ‘alaykum wa ji’tukum bi’āyatin min rabbikum fattaqū Allāha wa aṭī‘ūn.
And confirming the Torah that came before me, and to make lawful for you some of what was forbidden. I have come to you with a sign from your Lord, so fear Allah and obey me.
51
إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ رَبِّى وَرَبُّكُمْ فَٱعْبُدُوهُ ۗ هَـٰذَا صِرَٰطٌ مُّسْتَقِيمٌ
Inna Allāha rabbī wa rabbukum fa‘budūhu hādhā ṣirāṭun mustaqīm.
Indeed, Allah is my Lord and your Lord, so worship Him. This is the straight path.
52
فَلَمَّآ أَحَسَّ عِيسَىٰ مِنْهُمُ ٱلْكُفْرَ قَالَ مَنْ أَنصَارِىٓ إِلَى ٱللَّهِ ۖ قَالَ ٱلْحَوَارِيُّونَ نَحْنُ أَنصَارُ ٱللَّهِ...
Falammā aḥassa ‘Īsā min’humu al-kufra qāla man anṣārī ilā Allāh; qāla al-ḥawāriyyūna naḥnu anṣāru Allāh...
When Jesus sensed disbelief from them, he said, “Who are my supporters for Allah?” The disciples said, “We are the supporters of Allah.”
53
رَبَّنَآ ءَامَنَّا بِمَآ أَنزَلْتَ وَٱتَّبَعْنَا ٱلرَّسُولَ فَٱكْتُبْنَا مَعَ ٱلشَّـٰهِدِينَ
Rabbana āmannā bimā anzalta wa ittaba‘nā ar-rasūla fa’ktubnā ma‘a ash-shāhidīn.
Our Lord, we believe in what You revealed and follow the Messenger, so record us among the witnesses.
54
وَمَكَرُوا۟ وَمَكَرَ ٱللَّهُ ۖ وَٱللَّهُ خَيْرُ ٱلْمَـٰكِرِينَ
Wa makarū wa makar Allāh; wa Allāhu khayru al-mākirīn.
And they planned, but Allah planned. And Allah is the best of planners.
55
إِذْ قَالَ ٱللَّهُ يَـٰعِيسَىٰٓ إِنِّى مُتَوَفِّيكَ وَرَافِعُكَ إِلَىَّ...
Idh qāla Allāhu yā ‘Īsā innī mutawaffīka wa rāfi‘uka ilayya...
When Allah said, “O Jesus, I will take you and raise you to Myself...”
56
فَأَمَّا ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ فَأُعَذِّبُهُمْ...
Fa’ammā alladhīna kafarū fa u‘adhibuhum...
As for those who disbelieve, I will punish them severely in this world and the Hereafter...
57
وَأَمَّا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَعَمِلُوا۟ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ فَيُوَفِّيهِمْ أُجُورَهُمْ...
Wa ammā alladhīna āmanū wa ‘amilū aṣ-ṣāliḥāti fayuwaffīhim ujūrahum...
And as for those who believe and do good, He will give them their rewards in full...
58
ذَٰلِكَ نَتْلُوهُ عَلَيْكَ مِنَ ٱلْـَٔايَـٰتِ وَٱلذِّكْرِ ٱلْحَكِيمِ
Dhālika natlūhu ‘alayka mina al-āyāti wa adh-dhikri al-ḥakīm.
These are the verses and the wise reminder We recite to you.
59
إِنَّ مَثَلَ عِيسَىٰ عِندَ ٱللَّهِ كَمَثَلِ ءَادَمَ...
Inna mathala ‘Īsā ‘inda Allāhi kamathali Ādam...
Indeed, the example of Jesus in the sight of Allah is like that of Adam...
60
ٱلْحَقُّ مِن رَّبِّكَ فَلَا تَكُن مِّنَ ٱلْمُمْتَرِينَ
Al-ḥaqqu min rabbika falā takun mina al-mumtarīn.
The truth is from your Lord, so do not be among the doubters.
61
فَمَنْ حَاجَّكَ فِيهِ مِنۢ بَعْدِ مَا جَآءَكَ مِنَ ٱلْعِلْمِ...
Faman ḥājjaka fīhi min ba‘di mā jā’aka mina al-‘ilm...
If anyone disputes with you about him after this knowledge has come to you...
62
إِنَّ هَـٰذَا لَهُوَ ٱلْقَصَصُ ٱلْحَقُّ...
Inna hādhā lahuwa al-qaṣaṣu al-ḥaqq...
Indeed, this is the true narrative...
63
فَإِن تَوَلَّوْا۟ فَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ عَلِيمٌۢ بِٱلْمُفْسِدِينَ
Fa in tawallaw fa inna Allāha ‘alīmun bil-mufsidīn.
But if they turn away, then surely Allah knows well the corrupters.
64
قُلْ يَـٰٓأَهْلَ ٱلْكِتَـٰبِ تَعَالَوْا۟ إِلَىٰ كَلِمَةٍ سَوَآءٍ...
Qul yā ahla al-kitābi ta‘ālaw ilā kalimatin sawā’in...
Say, “O People of the Scripture, come to a common word between us and you...”
65
يَـٰٓأَهْلَ ٱلْكِتَـٰبِ لِمَ تُحَآجُّونَ فِىٓ إِبْرَٰهِـۧمَ...
Yā ahla al-kitābi lima tuḥājjūna fī Ibrāhīm...
O People of the Scripture, why do you argue about Abraham...
66
هَـٰٓأَنتُمْ هَـٰٓؤُلَآءِ حَـٰجَجْتُمْ فِيمَا لَكُم بِهِۦ عِلْمٌ...
Hā antum hāulā’i ḥājajtum fīmā lakum bihi ‘ilm...
Here you are — you argue about what you have knowledge of...
67
مَا كَانَ إِبْرَٰهِـۧمُ يَهُودِيًّا وَلَا نَصْرَانِيًّا...
Mā kāna Ibrāhīmu yahūdiyyan wa lā naṣrāniyyan...
Abraham was not a Jew nor a Christian, but he was a true Muslim submitting to Allah...
68
إِنَّ أَوْلَى ٱلنَّاسِ بِإِبْرَٰهِـۧمَ لَلَّذِينَ ٱتَّبَعُوهُ...
Inna awlā an-nāsi bi’Ibrāhīma lalladhīna ittaba‘ūhu...
Indeed, the people most worthy of Abraham are those who followed him...
69
وَدَّت طَّآئِفَةٌ مِّنْ أَهْلِ ٱلْكِتَـٰبِ لَوْ يُضِلُّونَكُمْ...
Waddat ṭā’ifatun min ahli al-kitābi law yuḍillūnakum...
A group from the People of the Scripture wished to mislead you...
70
يَـٰٓأَهْلَ ٱلْكِتَـٰبِ لِمَ تَكْفُرُونَ بِـَٔايَـٰتِ ٱللَّهِ...
Yā ahla al-kitābi lima takfurūna bi’āyāti Allāh...
O People of the Scripture! Why do you deny the signs of Allah while you witness them?
71
يَا أَهْلَ الْكِتَابِ لِمَ تَلْبِسُونَ الْحَقَّ بِالْبَاطِلِ وَتَكْتُمُونَ الْحَقَّ وَأَنْتُمْ تَعْلَمُونَ
Yā ahla al-kitābi lima talbisūna al-ḥaqqa bil-bāṭili wa taktumūna al-ḥaqqa wa antum ta‘lamūn
O People of the Scripture, why do you mix truth with falsehood and conceal the truth knowingly?
72
وَقَالَتْ طَائِفَةٌ مِنْ أَهْلِ الْكِتَابِ آمِنُوا بِالَّذِي أُنْزِلَ...
Wa qālat ṭā’ifatun min ahlil-kitābi āminū billadhī unzila...
A group among the People of the Scripture said, “Believe in what was revealed to the believers...”
73
وَلَا تُؤْمِنُوا إِلَّا لِمَنْ تَبِعَ دِينَكُمْ...
Wa lā tu’minū illā liman tabi‘a dīnakum...
“Do not believe except in those who follow your religion,” they say...
74
يَخْتَصُّ بِرَحْمَتِهِ مَنْ يَشَاءُ ۗ وَاللَّهُ ذُو الْفَضْلِ الْعَظِيمِ
Yakhtaṣṣu biraḥmatihi man yashā’ wallāhu dhūl-faḍlil-‘aẓīm
He chooses for His mercy whom He wills, and Allah is the possessor of great bounty.
75
وَمِنْ أَهْلِ الْكِتَابِ مَنْ إِنْ تَأْمَنْهُ بِقِنْطَارٍ...
Wa min ahlil-kitābi man in ta’manhu biqinṭārin...
Among the People of the Scripture are those whom if you entrust with a great treasure, they return it...
76
بَلَىٰ مَنْ أَوْفَىٰ بِعَهْدِهِ...
Balā man awfā bi‘ahdihi...
Yes, whoever fulfills his covenant and fears Allah — then He loves the righteous.
77
إِنَّ الَّذِينَ يَشْتَرُونَ بِعَهْدِ اللَّهِ...
Inna alladhīna yashtarūna bi‘ahdillāhi...
Indeed, those who exchange Allah’s covenant and oaths for a small price...
78
وَإِنَّ مِنْهُمْ لَفَرِيقًا يَلْوُونَ أَلْسِنَتَهُمْ...
Wa inna minhum la-farīqan yalwūna alsinatahum...
And indeed, among them is a group who distort words with their tongues...
79
مَا كَانَ لِبَشَرٍ أَنْ يُؤْتِيَهُ اللَّهُ الْكِتَابَ...
Mā kāna libasharin an yu’tiyahullāhu al-kitāba...
It is not for a human that Allah gives the Book and authority...
80
وَلَا يَأْمُرَكُمْ أَنْ تَتَّخِذُوا الْمَلَائِكَةَ...
Wa lā ya’murakum an tattakhidhū al-malā’ikata...
Nor would he order you to take angels and prophets as lords...
81
وَإِذْ أَخَذَ ٱللَّهُ مِيثَٰقَ ٱلنَّبِيِّۦنَ لَمَآ ءَاتَيْتُكُم مِّن كِتَٰبٍۢ وَحِكْمَةٍۢ ثُمَّ جَآءَكُمْ رَسُولٌ مُّصَدِّقٌۢ لِّمَا مَعَكُمْ لَتُؤْمِنُنَّ بِهِۦ وَلَتَنصُرُنَّهُۥ ۚ قَالَ ءَأَقْرَرْتُمْ وَأَخَذْتُمْ عَلَىٰ ذَٰلِكُمْ إِصْرِى ۖ قَالُوٓا۟ أَقْرَرْنَا ۚ قَالَ فَٱشْهَدُوا۟ وَأَنَا۠ مَعَكُم مِّنَ ٱلشَّٰهِدِينَ
Wa idh akhadhallāhu mīthāqan-nabiyyīna lamā ātaytukum min kitābin wa ḥikmah...
And when Allah took the covenant of the prophets, saying: If a messenger comes confirming what is with you, you must believe in him and support him...
82
فَمَن تَوَلَّىٰ بَعْدَ ذَٰلِكَ فَأُو۟لَٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلْفَٰسِقُونَ
Faman tawallā ba'da dhālika fa-ulā’ika humul-fāsiqūn
Then whoever turns away after this — they are the defiantly disobedient.
83
أَفَغَيْرَ دِينِ ٱللَّهِ يَبْغُونَ وَلَهُۥٓ أَسْلَمَ مَن فِى ٱلسَّمَٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ طَوْعًۭا وَكَرْهًۭا وَإِلَيْهِ يُرْجَعُونَ
Afa ghayra dīnillāhi yabghūna wa lahu aslama man fis-samāwāti wal-arḍ...
Do they seek a religion other than Allah’s, while to Him submits all in the heavens and the earth...
84
قُلْ ءَامَنَّا بِٱللَّهِ وَمَآ أُنزِلَ عَلَيْنَا...
Qul āmannā billāhi wa mā unzila 'alaynā...
Say: We believe in Allah and what has been revealed to us and what was revealed to Abraham...
85
وَمَن يَبْتَغِ غَيْرَ ٱلْإِسْلَٰمِ دِينًۭا فَلَن يُقْبَلَ مِنْهُ ۖ وَهُوَ فِى ٱلْـَٔاخِرَةِ مِنَ ٱلْخَٰسِرِينَ
Wa many-yabtaghi ghayral-islāmi dīnan falan yuqbala minhu...
Whoever seeks a religion other than Islam, it will never be accepted from him...
86
كَيْفَ يَهْدِى ٱللَّهُ قَوْمًۭا كَفَرُوا۟ بَعْدَ إِيمَٰنِهِمْ...
Kayfa yahdillāhu qawman kafarū ba‘da īmānihim...
How will Allah guide a people who disbelieved after they had believed...
87
أُو۟لَٰٓئِكَ جَزَآؤُهُمْ أَنَّ عَلَيْهِمْ لَعْنَةَ ٱللَّهِ...
Ulā’ika jazā’uhum anna 'alayhim la'natallāh...
Those — their reward is that upon them is the curse of Allah...
88
خَٰلِدِينَ فِيهَا لَا يُخَفَّفُ عَنْهُمُ ٱلْعَذَابُ...
Khālidīna fīhā lā yukhaffafu 'anhumul-'adhāb...
Abiding in it forever; the punishment will not be lightened for them...
89
إِلَّا ٱلَّذِينَ تَابُوا۟ مِنۢ بَعْدِ ذَٰلِكَ...
Illā alladhīna tābū min ba‘di dhālika...
Except for those who repent after that and reform — for indeed Allah is Forgiving, Merciful.
90
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ بَعْدَ إِيمَٰنِهِمْ ثُمَّ ٱزْدَادُوا۟ كُفْرًۭا لَّن تُقْبَلَ تَوْبَتُهُمْ...
Inna alladhīna kafarū ba'da īmānihim thumma izdādū kufrā...
Indeed, those who disbelieve after believing, then increase in disbelief — their repentance will never be accepted...
91
إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ وَمَاتُوا۟ وَهُمْ كُفَّارٌۭ فَلَن يُقْبَلَ مِنْ أَحَدِهِم مِّلْءُ ٱلْأَرْضِ ذَهَبًۭا...
Inna alladhīna kafarū wa mātū wa hum kuffār...
Indeed those who disbelieve and die while disbelievers — never will any amount of gold be accepted from them...
92
لَن تَنَالُوا۟ ٱلْبِرَّ حَتَّىٰ تُنفِقُوا۟ مِمَّا تُحِبُّونَ...
Lan tanālul-birra ḥattā tunfiqū mimmā tuḥibbūn...
You will never attain righteousness until you spend from what you love...
93
كُلُّ ٱلطَّعَامِ كَانَ حِلًّۭا لِّبَنِىٓ إِسْرَٰٓءِيلَ...
Kulluṭ-ṭa‘āmi kāna hillan li-banī isrā’īl...
All food was lawful for the Children of Israel except what Israel forbade for himself...
94
فَمَنِ ٱفْتَرَىٰ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ ٱلْكَذِبَ مِنۢ بَعْدِ ذَٰلِكَ...
Famani ftarā 'alallāhi l-kadhiba min ba‘di dhālika...
Whoever fabricates lies about Allah after this — they are the wrongdoers.
95
قُلْ صَدَقَ ٱللَّهُ ۗ فَٱتَّبِعُوا۟ مِلَّةَ إِبْرَٰهِيمَ حَنِيفًۭا...
Qul sadaqallāh fattabi‘ū millata ibrāhīma ḥanīfā...
Say: Allah has spoken the truth. So follow the way of Abraham, the upright...
96
إِنَّ أَوَّلَ بَيْتٍۢ وُضِعَ لِلنَّاسِ لَلَّذِى بِبَكَّةَ مُبَارَكًۭا...
Inna awwala baytin wuḍi‘a lin-nāsi lalladhī bibakkata mubārakā...
Indeed, the first House established for mankind was at Bakkah — blessed and a guidance for the worlds.
97
فِيهِ ءَايَٰتٌۭ بَيِّنَٰتٌۭ مَّقَامُ إِبْرَٰهِيمَ...
Fīhi āyātun bayyinātun maqāmu ibrāhīm...
In it are clear signs — the standing place of Abraham — and whoever enters it is safe...
98
قُلْ يَٰٓأَهْلَ ٱلْكِتَٰبِ لِمَ تَكْفُرُونَ بِـَٔايَٰتِ ٱللَّهِ...
Qul yā ahlal-kitābi lima takfurūna bi-āyātillāh...
Say: O People of the Book, why do you deny the signs of Allah while Allah witnesses what you do?
99
قُلْ يَٰٓأَهْلَ ٱلْكِتَٰبِ لِمَ تَصُدُّونَ عَن سَبِيلِ ٱللَّهِ...
Qul yā ahlal-kitābi lima taṣuddūna 'an sabīlillāh...
Say: O People of the Book, why do you turn people away from the path of Allah...
100
يَٰأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓا۟ إِن تُطِيعُوا۟ فَرِيقًۭا مِّنَ ٱلَّذِينَ أُوتُوا۟ ٱلْكِتَٰبَ...
Yā ayyuhalladhīna āmanū in tuṭī'ū farīqan mina alladhīna ūtul-kitāb...
O believers, if you obey a group of those who were given the Book, they will turn you back to disbelief after your faith.
101
وَكَيْفَ تَكْفُرُونَ وَأَنتُمْ تُتْلَىٰ عَلَيْكُمْ آيَاتُ اللَّهِ وَفِيكُمْ رَسُولُهُ ۗ وَمَنْ يَعْتَصِمْ بِاللَّهِ فَقَدْ هُدِيَ إِلَىٰ صِرَاطٍ مُسْتَقِيمٍ
And how could you disbelieve while Allah’s revelations are recited to you and His Messenger is among you? Whoever holds firmly to Allah is surely guided to the straight path.
102
يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا اتَّقُوا اللَّهَ حَقَّ تُقَاتِهِ وَلَا تَمُوتُنَّ إِلَّا وَأَنْتُمْ مُسْلِمُونَ
O believers! Be mindful of Allah as is His due, and do not die except in a state of submission.
103
وَاعْتَصِمُوا بِحَبْلِ اللَّهِ جَمِيعًا وَلَا تَفَرَّقُوا...
And hold firmly to the rope of Allah all together and do not become divided...
104
وَلْتَكُنْ مِنْكُمْ أُمَّةٌ يَدْعُونَ إِلَى الْخَيْرِ...
Let there be a group among you who call to goodness, enjoin what is right, and forbid what is wrong...
105
وَلَا تَكُونُوا كَالَّذِينَ تَفَرَّقُوا وَاخْتَلَفُوا...
And do not be like those who became divided and differed after clear proofs came to them...
106
يَوْمَ تَبْيَضُّ وُجُوهٌ وَتَسْوَدُّ وُجُوهٌ...
On that Day some faces will be bright and some faces will be dark...
107
وَأَمَّا الَّذِينَ ابْيَضَّتْ وُجُوهُهُمْ...
And as for those whose faces are bright, they will be in Allah’s mercy, remaining there forever.
108
تِلْكَ آيَاتُ اللَّهِ نَتْلُوهَا عَلَيْكَ بِالْحَقِّ...
These are Allah’s revelations. We recite them to you in truth. Allah does not wrong anyone.
109
وَلِلَّهِ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمَا فِي الْأَرْضِ...
To Allah belongs whatever is in the heavens and whatever is on the earth. To Him all matters return.
110
كُنْتُمْ خَيْرَ أُمَّةٍ أُخْرِجَتْ لِلنَّاسِ...
You are the best community raised for humanity: you encourage good, forbid evil, and believe in Allah...
111
لَنْ يَضُرُّوكُمْ إِلَّا أَذًى...
They will not harm you except for a little annoyance...
112
ضُرِبَتْ عَلَيْهِمُ الذِّلَّةُ أَيْنَ مَا ثُقِفُوا...
They are covered with humiliation wherever they are found except when they hold on to a covenant with Allah or with people...
113
لَيْسُوا سَوَاءً ۗ مِنْ أَهْلِ الْكِتَابِ...
They are not all alike. Among the People of the Book is an upright community...
114
يُؤْمِنُونَ بِاللَّهِ وَالْيَوْمِ الْآخِرِ...
They believe in Allah and the Last Day, enjoin what is right, forbid what is wrong, and hasten in good deeds...
115
وَمَا يَفْعَلُوا مِنْ خَيْرٍ فَلَنْ يُكْفَرُوهُ...
Whatever good they do will never be denied them. Allah knows the righteous.
116
إِنَّ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا لَنْ تُغْنِيَ عَنْهُمْ أَمْوَالُهُمْ...
Surely the wealth and children of the disbelievers will not help them against Allah at all...
117
مَثَلُ مَا يُنْفِقُونَ فِي هَٰذِهِ الْحَيَاةِ الدُّنْيَا...
The example of what they spend in this worldly life is like a cold wind that destroys a harvest...
118
يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا تَتَّخِذُوا بِطَانَةً...
O believers! Do not take outsiders into your inner circle, for they will spare no effort to corrupt you...
119
هَاأَنْتُمْ أُولَاءِ تُحِبُّونَهُمْ وَلَا يُحِبُّونَكُمْ...
Here you are! You love them, but they do not love you...
120
إِنْ تَمْسَسْكُمْ حَسَنَةٌ تَسُؤْهُمْ...
If something good happens to you, it upsets them; but if you are afflicted with harm, they rejoice...
121
وَإِذْ غَدَوْتَ مِنْ أَهْلِكَ تُبَوِّئُ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ مَقَاعِدَ لِلْقِتَالِ ۗ وَاللَّهُ سَمِيعٌ عَلِيمٌ
And ˹remember˺ when you left your family early in the morning to post the believers at their stations for battle. And Allah is All-Hearing, All-Knowing.
122
إِذْ هَمَّتْ طَائِفَتَانِ مِنْكُمْ أَنْ تَفْشَلَا وَاللَّهُ وَلِيُّهُمَا ۗ وَعَلَى اللَّهِ فَلْيَتَوَكَّلِ الْمُؤْمِنُونَ
When two groups among you were about to lose courage, but Allah was their Protector. So in Allah let the believers put their trust.

إِن يَنصُرْكُمُ ٱللَّهُ فَلَا غَالِبَ لَكُمْ ۖ وَإِن يَخْذُلْكُمْ فَمَن ذَا ٱلَّذِي يَنصُرُكُم مِنۢ بَعْدِهِۦ ۗ وَعَلَى ٱللَّهِ فَلْيَتَوَكَّلِ ٱلْمُؤْمِنُونَ ١٦٠
In yanṣurkumullāhu falā ghāliba lakum wa in yakhdhulkum faman dhā alladhī yanṣurukum min baʿdihi wa ʿalā Allāhi falyatawakkalil mu'minūn.
If Allah helps you, none can overcome you; but if He forsakes you, who then can help you? So in Allah let the believers put their trust.
161
وَمَا كَانَ لِنَبِيٍّ أَنْ يَغُلَّ ۚ وَمَنْ يَغْلُلْ يَأْتِ بِمَا غَلَّ يَوْمَ الْقِيَامَةِ ۚ ثُمَّ تُوَفَّىٰ كُلُّ نَفْسٍ مَا كَسَبَتْ وَهُمْ لَا يُظْلَمُونَ
Wa mā kāna linabiyyin an yaghulla; wa man yaghlul ya'ti bimā ghalla yawmal-qiyāmah, thumma tuwaffā kullu nafsin mā kasabat wa hum lā yuz'lamūn.
It is not for any prophet to act dishonestly. Whoever acts dishonestly will bring what he dishonestly took on the Day of Resurrection. Then every soul will be paid in full what it has earned, and they will not be wronged.
162
أَفَمَنِ اتَّبَعَ رِضْوَانَ اللَّهِ كَمَنْ بَاءَ بِسَخَطٍ مِنَ اللَّهِ وَمَأْوَاهُ جَهَنَّمُ ۚ وَبِئْسَ الْمَصِيرُ
Afa mani ittaba‘a ridwānallāhi kaman bā'a bisakhaṭim minallāh wa ma'wāhu jahannam; wa bi’sal-maṣīr.
Is one who follows the pleasure of Allah like one who earns the anger of Allah? His refuge is Hell, an evil destination.
163
هُمْ دَرَجَاتٌ عِنْدَ اللَّهِ ۗ وَاللَّهُ بَصِيرٌ بِمَا يَعْمَلُونَ
Hum darajātun ‘indallāh; wallāhu baṣīrun bimā ya‘malūn.
They are in varying degrees before Allah, and Allah sees what they do.
164
لَقَدْ مَنَّ اللَّهُ عَلَى الْمُؤْمِنِينَ إِذْ بَعَثَ فِيهِمْ رَسُولًا مِنْ أَنْفُسِهِمْ ...
Laqad manna’llāhu ‘alal-mu’minīna idh ba‘atha fīhim rasūlan min anfusihim...
Allah has surely blessed the believers when He sent among them a messenger of their own...
165
أَوَلَمَّا أَصَابَتْكُمْ مُصِيبَةٌ قَدْ أَصَبْتُمْ مِثْلَيْهَا ...
Awalammā aṣābatkum muṣībatun qad aṣabtum mith’laihā...
And was it so, when a disaster befell you though you had struck the twice of it...
166
وَمَا أَصَابَكُمْ يَوْمَ الْتَقَى الْجَمْعَانِ فَبِإِذْنِ اللَّهِ ...
Wa mā aṣābakum yawmal-taqal-jam‘āni fabi’idhnillāh...
What befell you the day the two hosts met was by Allah’s permission...
167
وَلِيَعْلَمَ الَّذِينَ نَافَقُوا ...
Wa liya‘lama alladhīna nāfaqū...
And that He might know the hypocrites...
168
الَّذِينَ قَالُوا لِإِخْوَانِهِمْ وَقَعَدُوا ...
Alladhīna qālū li’ikhwānihim wa qa‘adū...
Those who said of their brethren while they themselves sat at home...
169
وَلَا تَحْسَبَنَّ الَّذِينَ قُتِلُوا فِي سَبِيلِ اللَّهِ أَمْوَاتًا ...
Wa lā taḥsabanna alladhīna qutilū fī sabīlillāhi amwātā...
Think not of those who are slain in the way of Allah as dead...
170
فَرِحِينَ بِمَا آتَاهُمُ اللَّهُ مِنْ فَضْلِهِ ...
Fariḥīna bimā ātāhumullāhu min faḍlihī...
Rejoicing in what Allah has bestowed upon them of His bounty...
171
يَسْتَبْشِرُونَ بِنِعْمَةٍ مِنَ اللَّهِ وَفَضْلٍ ...
Yastabshirūna bini‘matin minallāhi wa faḍlin...
They rejoice in grace from Allah and bounty, and that Allah wastes not the reward of believers.
172
الَّذِينَ اسْتَجَابُوا لِلَّهِ وَالرَّسُولِ ...
Alladhīna istajābū lillāhi wal-rasūli...
Those who responded to Allah and the Messenger after the hurt had befallen them...
173
الَّذِينَ قَالَ لَهُمُ النَّاسُ إِنَّ النَّاسَ قَدْ جَمَعُوا لَكُمْ ...
Alladhīna qāla lahumun-nāsu innan-nāsa qad jama‘ū lakum...
Those unto whom men said: The people have gathered against you, therefore fear them...
174
فَانْقَلَبُوا بِنِعْمَةٍ مِنَ اللَّهِ وَفَضْلٍ ...
Fanqalabū bini‘matin minallāhi wa faḍlin...
So they returned with grace and bounty from Allah. No harm touched them...
175
إِنَّمَا ذَلِكُمُ الشَّيْطَانُ يُخَوِّفُ أَوْلِيَاءَهُ ...
Innamā dhālikumush-shayṭānu yukhawwifu awliyā'ahū...
It is only Satan who frightens his allies, so fear them not, but fear Me, if you are believers.
176
وَلَا يَحْزُنْكَ الَّذِينَ يُسَارِعُونَ فِي الْكُفْرِ ...
Wa lā yaḥzunka alladhīna yusāri‘ūna fil-kufr...
Let not those grieve you who rush into disbelief...
177
إِنَّ الَّذِينَ اشْتَرَوُا الْكُفْرَ بِالْإِيمَانِ ...
Inna alladhīna ishtarawul-kufra bil-īmān...
Those who purchase disbelief at the price of faith harm Allah not at all...
178
وَلَا يَحْسَبَنَّ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا أَنَّمَا نُمْلِي لَهُمْ ...
Wa lā yaḥsabanna alladhīna kafarū annamā numlī lahum...
And let not those who disbelieve think that Our granting them respite is good for them...
179
مَا كَانَ اللَّهُ لِيَذَرَ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ عَلَىٰ مَا أَنْتُمْ عَلَيْهِ ...
Mā kānallāhu liyadharal-mu’minīna ‘alā mā antum ‘alayh...
Allah is not about to leave the believers in the state you are in...
180
وَلَا يَحْسَبَنَّ الَّذِينَ يَبْخَلُونَ بِمَا آتَاهُمُ اللَّهُ مِنْ فَضْلِهِ ...
Wa lā yaḥsabanna alladhīna yabkhalūna bimā ātāhumullāhu min faḍlihī...
And let not those who hoard what Allah has given them think it is good for them...
181
لَقَدْ سَمِعَ اللَّهُ قَوْلَ الَّذِينَ قَالُوا إِنَّ اللَّهَ فَقِيرٌ وَنَحْنُ أَغْنِيَاءُ ۘ سَنَكْتُبُ مَا قَالُوا وَقَتْلَهُمُ الْأَنْبِيَاءَ بِغَيْرِ حَقٍّ ۚ وَنَقُولُ ذُوقُوا عَذَابَ الْحَرِيقِ
Laqad sami‘a Allahu qawla alladhīna qālū inna Allāha faqīrun wa naḥnu aghniyā’. Sanaktubu mā qālū wa qatlahumu l-anbiyā’a bighayri ḥaqq, wa naqūlu dhūqū ‘adhāba l-ḥarīq.
Allah has certainly heard the statement of those who said, “Indeed, Allah is poor while we are rich.” We will record what they said, and their killing of the prophets unjustly. And We will say, “Taste the punishment of the Burning Fire.”
182
ذَٰلِكَ بِمَا قَدَّمَتْ أَيْدِيكُمْ وَأَنَّ اللَّهَ لَيْسَ بِظَلَّامٍ لِلْعَبِيدِ
Dhālika bimā qaddamat aydīkum wa anna Allāha laysa biẓallāmin lil‘abīd.
That is because of what your hands have sent ahead, and Allah is not unjust to His servants.
183
الَّذِينَ قَالُوا إِنَّ اللَّهَ عَهِدَ إِلَيْنَا أَلَّا نُؤْمِنَ لِرَسُولٍ حَتَّى يَأْتِيَنَا بِقُرْبَانٍ تَأْكُلُهُ النَّارُ ۗ قُلْ قَدْ جَاءَكُمْ رُسُلٌ مِنْ قَبْلِي بِالْبَيِّنَاتِ وَبِالَّذِي قُلْتُمْ فَلِمَ قَتَلْتُمُوهُمْ إِنْ كُنْتُمْ صَادِقِينَ
Alladhīna qālū inna Allāha ‘ahida ilaynā allā nu’mina lirasūlin ḥattā ya’tiyanā biqurbānin ta’kuluhu n-nār...
Those who said, “Allah has commanded us not to believe in a messenger unless he brings us an offering consumed by fire.” Say, “Messengers before me came to you with proofs and with what you requested. Why then did you kill them, if you are truthful?”
184
فَإِنْ كَذَّبُوكَ فَقَدْ كُذِّبَ رُسُلٌ مِنْ قَبْلِكَ جَاءُوا بِالْبَيِّنَاتِ وَالزُّبُرِ وَالْكِتَابِ الْمُنِيرِ
Fa-in kadhdhabūka faqad kudhdhiba rusulun min qablika jā’ū bil-bayyināti wa z-zuburi wa l-kitābi l-munīr.
If they deny you, then messengers before you were also denied, who came with clear proofs, scriptures, and the enlightening Book.
185
كُلُّ نَفْسٍ ذَائِقَةُ الْمَوْتِ ۗ وَإِنَّمَا تُوَفَّوْنَ أُجُورَكُمْ يَوْمَ الْقِيَامَةِ...
Kullu nafsin dhā’iqatul-mawt, wa innamā tuwaffawna ujurakum yawma l-qiyāmah...
Every soul shall taste death. And you will only be given your full reward on the Day of Resurrection...
186
لَتُبْلَوُنَّ فِي أَمْوَالِكُمْ وَأَنْفُسِكُمْ...
Latublawunna fī amwālikum wa anfusikum...
You will certainly be tested in your possessions and yourselves...
187
وَإِذْ أَخَذَ اللَّهُ مِيثَاقَ الَّذِينَ أُوتُوا الْكِتَابَ...
Wa idh akhadha Allahu mīthāqa alladhīna ūtū l-kitāba...
And when Allah took a covenant from those who were given the Scripture...
188
لَا تَحْسَبَنَّ الَّذِينَ يَفْرَحُونَ بِمَا أَتَوْا...
Lā taḥsabanna alladhīna yafraḥūna bimā ataw...
Do not think those who rejoice in what they have done...
189
وَلِلَّهِ مُلْكُ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ ۚ وَاللَّهُ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ
Wa lillāhi mulkus-samāwāti wa l-arḍ, wa Allāhu ‘alā kulli shay’in qadīr.
To Allah belongs the dominion of the heavens and the earth, and Allah is over all things competent.
190
إِنَّ فِي خَلْقِ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ...
Inna fī khalqi s-samāwāti wa l-arḍ...
Indeed, in the creation of the heavens and the earth...
191
الَّذِينَ يَذْكُرُونَ اللَّهَ قِيَامًا وَقُعُودًا...
Alladhīna yadhkurūna Allāha qiyāman wa qu‘ūdan...
Those who remember Allah while standing, sitting, and lying on their sides...
192
رَبَّنَا إِنَّكَ مَنْ تُدْخِلِ النَّارَ فَقَدْ أَخْزَيْتَهُ...
Rabbana innaka man tudkhili n-nāra faqad akhzaytahu...
Our Lord, indeed whoever You admit to the Fire, You have disgraced him...
193
رَبَّنَا إِنَّنَا سَمِعْنَا مُنَادِيًا يُنَادِي لِلْإِيمَانِ...
Rabbana innanā sami‘nā munādiyan yunādī lil-īmān...
Our Lord, indeed we have heard a caller calling to faith...
194
رَبَّنَا وَآتِنَا مَا وَعَدْتَنَا عَلَى رُسُلِكَ...
Rabbana wa ātinā mā wa‘adtanā ‘alā rusulik...
Our Lord, grant us what You promised us through Your messengers...
195
فَاسْتَجَابَ لَهُمْ رَبُّهُمْ أَنِّي لَا أُضِيعُ عَمَلَ عَامِلٍ مِنْكُمْ...
Fa-istajāba lahum rabbuhum annī lā uḍī‘u ‘amala ‘āmilin minkum...
So their Lord answered them, “I will never allow the deeds of any worker among you to be lost...”
196
لَا يَغُرَّنَّكَ تَقَلُّبُ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا فِي الْبِلَادِ
Lā yaghurrannaka taqallubu alladhīna kafarū fī l-bilād.
Do not be deceived by the prosperity of the disbelievers throughout the land.
197
مَتَاعٌ قَلِيلٌ ثُمَّ مَأْوَاهُمْ جَهَنَّمُ ۚ وَبِئْسَ الْمِهَادُ
Matā‘un qalīlun thumma ma’wāhum jahannam, wa bi’sa l-mihād.
A brief enjoyment; then their final refuge is Hell, and wretched is the resting place.
198
لَكِنِ الَّذِينَ اتَّقَوْا رَبَّهُمْ لَهُمْ جَنَّاتٌ...
Lākin alladhīna ttaqaw rabbahum lahum jannāt...
But those who fear their Lord will have gardens beneath which rivers flow...
199
وَإِنَّ مِنْ أَهْلِ الْكِتَابِ لَمَنْ يُؤْمِنُ بِاللَّهِ...
Wa inna min ahli l-kitābi laman yu’minu billāh...
And indeed, among the People of the Book are those who believe in Allah...
200
يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا اصْبِرُوا وَصَابِرُوا...
Yā ayyuhā alladhīna āmanū iṣbirū wa ṣābirū...
O you who believe, be patient, persevere, stand firm, and fear Allah so that you may succeed.
