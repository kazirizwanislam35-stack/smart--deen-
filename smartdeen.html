About Smart Deen

Our Vision

Smart Deen is a modern Islamic digital platform created with one goal: to guide, inspire, and educate the youth with authentic Islamic knowledge in a simple, beautiful, and accessible way.

Founder

My name is Kazi Rizwan Islam. I created Smart Deen with the dream of helping the youth learn Islam with love, clarity, and consistency. Inspired by Abu Saad Tauha Ibn Jalil Munawar Zama, I decided to begin a journey of making Islamic learning easy and enjoyable for everyone.

The Mission

Smart Deen will slowly grow into a full learning platform— with Islamic notes, Qur’an, Hadith, Namaz guides, Duas, Stories of the Prophets, reminders, and even future Islamic lectures and courses.

The Team

This project is supported by my brothers and friends: Farhan Ali and Ariyan Ali. With their help, and with the support of powerful tools like AI, technology, and research, Smart Deen will become a platform for the whole Ummah.

Our Promise

We will continue upgrading Smart Deen with:

  • Daily Islamic content
  • Guided learning
  • New Smart Deen tools
  • Islamic productivity features
  • Beautiful Sunnah reminders
All while staying true to the Smart Deen green theme, simplicity, and excellence.
